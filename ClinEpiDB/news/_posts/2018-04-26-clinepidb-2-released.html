We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 2

New Features in this Release

  • A new tutorial explains the options ClinEpiDB provides to explore results after performing a search. These include the dynamic tabular outputs, column visualization tools, and data download options.
  • In the Distributions app we have added automatically generated summary statistics: mean, median, range, SD (standard deviation) & interquartile range (IQR)
  • Improvements to our axis labels in our analysis apps
  • Implementation of indicators of available data. Filters are now annotated with eg. 1 of 44,508 Observations have no data provided for this filter.
  • Improvements to Active Filters summary popup describing more clearly filter selections, especially in the Related Observations step.

No new Data in this Release
