We are pleased to announce the release of AmoebaDB 48.

The product of 6 months of back-end infrastructure work, Release 47 includes beta.amoebadb.org in addition to our legacy sites. The beta site contains the same data as the legacy site but also has new tools and functions in an updated and streamlined interface that emphasizes easy access to tools and help information. For example, the search functionality in the header now performs a site-wide search.
