We are pleased to announce the first release of ClinEpiDB

This release of ClinEpiDB contains data from the Program for Resistance, Immunology, Surveillance and Modeling of Malaria in Uganda (PRISM) study. PRISM is the dedicated East Africa International Center of Excellence for Malaria Research. The data presented here include the longitudinal cohort and entomological surveillance components of the PRISM study.

New Features

  • The contingency table app allows you to create 2x2 contingency tables of different categorical variables or allows you to create categorical variables from continuous measurements. Here you can explore the relationship between two variables.
  • The data summary app for longitudinal datasets, allows you to plot summary information, similar to a box-and-whisker plot for variables over time. The timeline(s) allow you to filter by time periods and age groups of interest.
  • The Distributions app allows you to visualize histograms of available variables and then facet or stratify these distributions by additional variablaes.
