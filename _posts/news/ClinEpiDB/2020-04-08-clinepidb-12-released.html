We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 12!

New Features in this Release

  • You can now filter studies by category! We have added a drop-down box to the right of the Search box on the homepage that allows you to select which studies you are interested in. We currently have enteric disease, malaria, and schistosomiasis data available for exploration and download.

New Data in this Release

  • Our fourth dataset from the Schistosomiasis Consortium for Operational Research and Evaluation, the SCORE Zanzibar S. haematobium Cluster Randomized Trial aimed to assess biannual mass drug administration (MDA) applied alone or with complementary snail control or behaviour change interventions for the reduction of Schistosoma haematobium prevalence and infection intensity in children from Zanzibar and to compare the effect between the clusters. The primary outcome was S. haematobium infection prevalence and intensity in 9-12-year-old children after 5 years of follow-up.
  • New MAL-ED data! The initial data release of the Etiology, Risk Factors, and Interactions of Enteric Infections and Malnutrition and the Consequences for Child Health and Development (MAL-ED) study occurred in March of 2019. This longitudinal cohort study was conducted at eight sites across three continents: Bangladesh, Nepal, India, Pakistan, Tanzania, South Africa, Peru and Brazil. This release adds vaccine, gut function and an additional 3 years of data for a majority of MAL-ED participants.
