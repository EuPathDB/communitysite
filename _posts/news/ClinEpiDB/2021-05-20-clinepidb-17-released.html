We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 17!

New Features

  • We're making it easier for study teams to control access to their data! Now study teams can log in, access the "Data Access Dashboard" from their study page, and give others access to their data, either as part of the study team or as an external collaborator (End User).

New Data

  • The Performance of Rotavirus and Oral Polio Vaccines in Developing Countries (PROVIDE) study has undergone some minor data updates. Data on a handful of blood and urine samples has been added.

Webinars

  • We're wrapping up our webinar series on infectious disease epidemiology and maternal, newborn, and child health epidemiology! Catch our last webinar on Tuesday, May 25th at 2 PM UTC on an observational study of preterm birth outcomes in Ethiopia by registering here, or see recordings of our past webinars here.
