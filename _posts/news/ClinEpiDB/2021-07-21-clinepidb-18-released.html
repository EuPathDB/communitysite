We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 18!

New Features

  • Check out the new "Help" menu in our website header. No matter where you are or what data you're looking at, you'll be able to quickly jump to pages like "Tutorials" or "External Resources".
  • We are working hard to revamp the ClinEpiDB website, making it easier to move between browsing the data and creating visualizations, and adding new features like the ability to create custom lists of your own "favorite" variables. Follow us on Facebook or Twitter to get sneak peeks of these new features, and keep an eye out for the new website this fall!

New Data

  • The India ICEMR Meghalaya Cross-sectional study was conducted by the Center for the Study of Complex Malaria in India (CSCMi). Data is now available from over 3,000 participants who were surveyed in 2018 and 2019 to assess individual and village-level patterns of Plasmodium infection in northeastern India.

Webinars

  • We hosted a webinar series on infectious disease and maternal, newborn, and child health epidemiology this Spring. If you missed any webinars, you can still access the recordings here.
