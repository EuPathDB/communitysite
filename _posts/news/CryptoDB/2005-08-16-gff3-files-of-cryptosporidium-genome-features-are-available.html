--- layout: post title: GFF3 Files of Cryptosporidium Genome Features are Available category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,CryptoDB] --- CryptoDB is one of NIAID's Bioinformatics Resource Centers (BRC) for Biodefense and Emerging or Re-Emerging Infectious Diseases. As part of our efforts for interoperability among the other BRCs and the research community in general we have released the published Cryptosporidium genome features in standardized GFF3 format. The files may be downloaded via FTP from ftp://ftp.tigr.org/pub/brc/ApiDB/Cryptosporidium/.
