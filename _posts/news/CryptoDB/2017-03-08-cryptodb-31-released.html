We are pleased to announce the release of CryptoDB 31

New Data in Release 31

Annotated Genome

Cryptosporidium ubiquitum isolate 39726 — isolated in 2013 from an 12-year-old female patient in the USA, this organism's annotated genome appears for the first time in CryptoDB.
Genbank Bioproject PRJNA246478. This data is provided pre-publication by Lihua Xiao of the CDC, Atlanta GA.
