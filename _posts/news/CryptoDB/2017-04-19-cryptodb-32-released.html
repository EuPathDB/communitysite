We are pleased to announce the release of CryptoDB 32

New Data in Release 32

Annotated Genomes

  • Cryptosporidium andersoni isolate 30847 — Cryptosporidium andersoni 30847 was isolated from beef cattle in Alberta Canada in 2009. This organism's annotated genome appears for the first time in CryptoDB. Genome home page at NCBI/GenBank. Liu et al.
  • Cryptosporidium hominis TU502_2012 — Cryptosporidium hominis isolate TU502_2012, isolated in 2012 at Tufts University (USA) was a result of multiple (approximately 20) passages of the original TU502 isolate (2004) in gnotobiotic pigs. NCBI Bioproject home page. Ifeonu et al.

Transcriptomics

  • Transcriptome of 48 hours in vitro infection — Transcriptome (RNA sequence) from Cryptosporidium parvum Iowa isolated from an in vitro infection of cultured HCT-8 cells. Isaza et al.
  • Transcriptome of oocyst and intracellular stages — Cryptosporidium parvum transcriptome from intracellular (24 h post-infection) and oocysts Stages. Data provided pre-publication by Giovanni Widmer, Tufts University

No New features in this release

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
