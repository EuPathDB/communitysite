New data in CryptoDB 46

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Cryptosporidium parvum strain IOWA ATCC —
    Based on PacBio and Illumina sequencing of strain IOWA ATCC by the Wellcome Trust Sanger Center with annotation from the Kissinger group at University of Georgia. The sequence and annotation has been submitted to the NCBI GenBank under accession numbers (Bioproject: PRJNA573722, Biosample: SAMN12822784, WGS: CP044415-CP044422) The data is provided to CryptoDB pre-publication.

Whole Genome Sequencing of Isolates (Genetic Variation)

  • Cryptosporidium parvum WGS of clinical isolates —
    Whole genome sequence of C. parvum isolates determined by an IMS/Illumina-based method for the WGS of clinical isolates. Nader et al.

New features in CryptoDB 46

  • Copy Number Variation Pipeline Bug Fix: To correct skewed copy number predictions, we changed the method for counting reads in our CNV pipeline from Cufflinks to HTSeq-Count. In our new pipeline we calculate FPKM manually. Please note - saved strategies that include CNV searches will likely produce different results in Release 46.
  • Our pipeline of genomic analyses includes open reading frames (ORFs) prediction in addition to loading gene models from the official annotation. Because data usage analysis showed low ORF usage, in our next release (47) predicted ORFs will be available as a genome-scale fasta file from our downloads section and removed from the searches and genome browser.
