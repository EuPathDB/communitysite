New data in PlasmoDB 46

Antibody Array

  • Peptide arrays of individuals naturally exposed to Plasmodium – Not Ready (Oct 21). Jaenisch et al.

ChIP Seq

  • ATAC-seq of the intraerythrocytic development cycle – ATAC-seq analysis of tightly synchronized parasites (Pfal3D7 10G and 1.2B subclones) at four different developmental stages during the IDC: early rings (10-15 h, T10), late rings (20-25 h, T20), trophozoites (30-35 h, T30) and schizonts (40-45 h, T40). Ruiz et al.
  • Midgut oocysts and salivary gland sporozoite epigenetics – ChIP-seq analyses of histone modifications, including H3K9ac, H3K27ac, H3K4me3, and H3K9me3, at the oocyst and sporozoite stages.. Gomez-Diaz et al.
  • Histone epigenome of Plasmodium vivax salivary-gland sporozoites – Histone epigenome of Plasmodium vivax salivary-gland sporozoites determined by ChIP Seq analysis via H3K9me3, H3K4me3, H3K9ac, H4K16ac chromatin precipitations.. Vivax Sporozoite Consortium et al.

RNA Sequence

  • Dual transcriptomes of malaria-infected Gambian children – Parasite portion of a dual transcriptomic analysis of human and Plasmodium from blood of 46 Gambian children diagnosed with malaria. Lee et al. The host component of this data set is available on HostDB: Transcriptomes of 46 malaria-infected Gambian children.
  • P. berghei transcriptome during inducible gametocytogenesis – A chart of the sequence of transcriptional changes through gametocyte development, including the observation that gender-specific transcription occurred within 6 h of induction. Kent et al.
  • Transcriptome of P. vivax salivary gland sporozoites – Transcriptomic analysis via RNA sequencing of P. vivax salivary gland sporozoites from isolates of symptomatic blood-stage malaria infections. Vivax Sporozoite Consortium et al.

New features in PlasmoDB 46

  • Copy Number Variation Pipeline Bug Fix: To correct skewed copy number predictions, we changed the method for counting reads in our CNV pipeline from Cufflinks to HTSeq-Count. In our new pipeline we calculate FPKM manually. Please note - saved strategies that include CNV searches will likely produce different results in Release 46.
  • Our pipeline of genomic analyses includes open reading frames (ORFs) prediction in addition to loading gene models from the official annotation. Because data usage analysis showed low ORF usage, in our next release (47) predicted ORFs will be available as a genome-scale fasta file from our downloads section and removed from the searches and genome browser.
