--- layout: post title: FungiDB 1.0 beta category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,FungiDB] --- We are pleased to announce the release of FungiDB 1.0 beta


This release of FungiDB includes 18 genomes of 17 species of Fungi in Ascomycete, Basidiomycete, and Zygomycota
The following Fungal Genomes are now available via FungiDB:
  • Eurotiomycetes; Ascomycota
    • Aspergillus clavatus
    • Aspergillus flavus
    • Aspergillus fumigatus strain Af293
    • Aspergillus niger
    • Aspergillus nidulans (Emericella nidulans) strain A4
    • Aspergillus terreus
    • Coccidoidies immitis strain RS
    • Coccidoidies immitis strain H538.4

  • Sordariomycetes; Ascomyocta
    • Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. lycopersici
    • Gibberella zeae (Fusarium graminearum)
    • Gibberella moniliformis (Fusarium verticillioides)
    • Magnaporthea oryzae formerly (Magnaporthe grisea)
    • Neurospora crassa strain OR74A

  • Saccharomycotina; Ascomyocta
    • Candida albicans
    • Saccharomyces cerevisiae

  • Basidiomycota
    • Filobasidiella neoformans (Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii) strain H99
    • Puccinia graminis

  • Mucormycotina ("Zygomycota")
    • Rhizopus oryzae
