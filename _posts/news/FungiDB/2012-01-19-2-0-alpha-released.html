--- layout: post title: 2.0 alpha released category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,FungiDB] --- The 2.0 alpha release, with 8 genomes, from 5 Cryptococcus, Tremella mesenterica, Neurospora crassa, and Saccharomyces cerevisiae were released at alpha.fungidb.org. This is a pre-release using new workflow and data analysis pipeline. It will soon follow with additional species that incorporate the previously released genomes.
