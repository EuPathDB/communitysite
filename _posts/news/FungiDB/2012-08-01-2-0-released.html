--- layout: post title: 2.0 released category: [FungiDB] --- The 2.0 release, with 31 genomes including 6 Oomycetes was released at fungidb.org.The release includes improved view of the lists of species, links to OrthoMCL, inclusion of Microarray and RNA-Seq datasets from C.albicans, S. cerevisiae, N. crassa, S.pombe, and P. ramorum. The inclusion of the Oomycetes represents a collaboration with Brett Tyler's group at Oregon State University. This is the first full release using new workflow and data analysis pipeline that has incorporated automation into the system. This release of FungiDB includes 31 genomes of Fungi and Oomycetes.
