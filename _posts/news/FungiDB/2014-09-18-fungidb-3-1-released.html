--- layout: post title: FungiDB 3.1 released category: [FungiDB] --- FungiDB 3.1 is released this month.

Updates

14 RNA-Seq datasets are added for a total of 22 datasets.

2 Microarray datasets are now available.

Corrections

SC25 of Cryptococcus gattii R265 - SC25 changed to mitochondrial

Corrected the exon phase interpretation of several JGI genomes:

  • Aspergillus aculeatus ATCC 16872
  • Mucor circinelloides f. lusitanicus
  • Phycomyces blakesleeanus NRRL 1555(-)
  • Phytophthora capsici
  • Phanerochaete chrysosporium
  • Phytophthora ramorum Pr-102
  • Pythium ultimum DAOM BR144
  • Tremella mesenterica DSM 1558

Known issues

HTS SNPs remain unavailable until the next point release.
