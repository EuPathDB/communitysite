We are pleased to announce the release of FungiDB 25

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Aspergillus oryzae RIB40 — AspGD version 13 April 2015. Genome sequence and annotation for this organism are available for the first time in FungiDB.

Curated Phenotypes

  • Curated Cryptococcus Phenotypes — Phenotypes curated from the literature by Diane Inglis are available in tabulated form on the gene pages of 446 genes from Cryptococcus gattii strains WM276 and R265, Cryptpcoccus neoformans var. neoformans JEC21, var. grubii H99, and var. neoformans B-3501A.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
