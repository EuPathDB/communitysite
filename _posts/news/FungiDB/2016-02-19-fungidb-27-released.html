We are pleased to announce the release of FungiDB 27

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

RNA Sequencing

The following RNA sequencing data was originally loaded into FungiDB in Sept 2013 but erroneously contained several A. nidulans samples that were aligned to N. crassa. The A. nidulans data and N. crassa data are now two separate data sets in FungiDB.
  • Inducer-Free Cellulase Secretion in Neurospora crassa — Global mRNA abundances from liquid cultures of N. crassa were measured by sequencing on the Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx and HiSeq2000 platforms. Cultures were harvested at 4 hours, after a switch to media of interest. Coradetti et al.
  • Inducer-Free Cellulase Secretion in Aspergillus nidulans — Global mRNA abundances from liquid cultures of A. nidulans were measured by sequencing on the Illumina Genome Analyzer IIx and HiSeq2000 platforms. Cultures were harvested at 6 hours, after a switch to media of interest. Coradetti et al.

New Features

New Tutorials

  • Exploring expression distributions with our Percentile Ranking searches - Percentile ranking is one way that EuPathDB analyzes expression data. This tutorial describes our percentile ranking of expression data and the searches that we offer based on that analysis.
  • Finding Differentially expressed genes based on Fold Change with a P-value - One way EuPathDB analyzes expression is to determine statistically significant differential gene expression using the Fisher's exact test. This tutorial describes our Fold Change w/ P-value searches that return differentially expressed genes based on the level of statistical confidence that you choose.
