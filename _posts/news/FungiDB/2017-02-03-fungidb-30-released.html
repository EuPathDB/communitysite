We are pleased to announce the release of FungiDB 30

New data in this release

Genomes:

The following annotated genomes appear for the first time in FungiDB. Munoz et al.

  • Paracoccidioides brasiliensis Pb18 – Sequenced at the Broad Institute and integrated into FungiDB as Genbank version Oct 24, 2014.
  • Paracoccidioides brasiliensis Pb03 – Sequenced at the Broad Institute and integrated into FungiDB as Genbank version Nov 13, 2014.
  • Paracoccidioides lutzii Pb01 – Sequenced at the Broad Institute and integrated into FungiDB as Genbank version Nov 03, 2014.

RNA Sequencing:

  • Transcriptome induction by five crop straws – A transcriptomic analysis of Neurospora crassa using five major crop residues. Wang et al.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
