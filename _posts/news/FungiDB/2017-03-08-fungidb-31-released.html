We are pleased to announce the release of FungiDB 31

New data in this release

Annotated Genomes:

  • Fusarium fujikuroi strain IMI 58289 – appearing for the first time in FungiDB. Wiemann et al.
  • Fusarium graminearum strain PH-1 – appearing for the first time in FungiDB. King et al.

RNA Sequencing:

  • Cell cycle transcriptome of Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii – Cell-cycle transcript dynamics of wild-type budding yeast Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii. Kelliher et al.
  • Cell cycle transcriptome of Saccharomyces cerevisiae S288C – Cell-cycle transcript dynamics of wild-type budding yeast Saccharomyces cerevisiae. Kelliher et al.
  • Transcriptome during sexual development in Neurospora crassa OR74A – Genome-wide transcriptomics of Neurospora crassa across eight stages of sexual development. Lehr et al.
  • Transcriptome of Neurospora discreta during sexual development – Genome-wide transcriptomics of Neurospora discreta across eight stages of sexual development. Lehr et al.
  • Transcriptome of Neurospora tetrasperma during sexual development – Genome-wide transcriptomics of Neurospora tetrasperma across eight stages of sexual development. Lehr et al.

Proteomics:

  • Phosphopeptide enriched sample (strain A1166) – Aspergillus nidulans Phosphoproteome. Ramsubramaniam et al.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - C. albicans clinical isolates – Whole genome sequencing data from multiple C. albicans isolates collected from patients wtih oropharyngeal candidiasis. NCBI's Bioproject record page and Ford et al.
  • Aligned genome sequence reads : Multiple Coccidioides Isolates – Coccidioides whole population genome SNP analysis reveals local population structure and patterns of dispersal in the Western Hemisphere. NCBI's Bioproject record page

Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, EC numbers, gene names, synonyms, publications and notes from curators.

Neurospora Broad Community Annotation Project

Annotations from the Broad Community Annotation Project have been loaded for Neurospora crassa OR74A. Updates include publications, EC numbers, GO terms, gene mappings and MIPS annotations (converted to GO terms). Raw dumps from Broad are available as are parsed and cleaned tab files.
