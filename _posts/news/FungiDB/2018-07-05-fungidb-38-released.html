We are pleased to announce the release of FungiDB 38

New data in FungiDB 38

Genome Sequence and Annotation: these annotated genomes appear for the first time in FungiDB.

  • Cryptococcus gattii EJB2 – Farrer et al.
  • Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. melonis 26406 – Ma et al.
  • Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. cubense race 1 – Guo et al.
  • Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. cubense race 4 – Guo et al.
  • Histoplasma capsulatum H143 – NCBI BioProject PRJNA29161

Genome Sequence and Annotation: updated genomes

  • Aspergillus campestris IBT 28561 – Kjærbølling et al.
  • Aspergillus novofumigatus IBT 16806 – Kjærbølling et al.
  • Aspergillus steynii IBT 23096 – Kjærbølling et al.

Transcriptomics

  • Gene expression changes during mycelia differentiation in Coccidioides immitis – A custom open reading frame oligonucleotide microarray to compare the transcriptome of C. immitis mycelia with two spherule samples grown in vitro. Viriyakosol et al.
  • Comparative transcriptomics of C. neoformans mutants defective in heterochromatin regulation – Stranded RNA-Seq profiles of WT, knockout strains of ezh2, msl1, ccc1, clr4, and ccc1-W52A aromatic cage mutant. Dumesic et al.
  • Stage-specific and genome-wide gene expression during the different developmental stages of Fusarium graminearum – Stand specific RNA-Seq of F. graminerum growth stages (conidia, vegetative hyphae, and perithecia at 6dpi) Liu et al.
  • Transcriptomic analysis during vegetative and infectious growth of Fusarium graminearum PH-1 – RNA-Seq of F. graminearum mycelia and 6dpi infection samples assessed by paired-end deep Illumina sequencing. Wang et al.
  • N. crassa transcriptomes during vegetative and sexual development stage – Strand-specific RNA-Seq of Neurospora crassa growth stages Liu et al.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - Genomic context of azole-resistance mutations in Aspergillus fumigatus – Whole-genome sequencing (WGS) was performed for high-resolution single-nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) analysis of 24 A. fumigatus isolates (azole-resistant and susceptible strains). Abdolrasouli et al.

Sequence sites, features and motifs

  • Coccidioides immitis RS TE GBrowse track – Annotation of transposable elements (TE) in Coccidioides immitis RS using RepeatMasker which identified TEs based on the RepBase reference library and REPET to support de novo TE identification. Kirkland et al.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
