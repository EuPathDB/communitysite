Genome Sequence and Annotation: updated genomes

  • Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii KN99 – Gene mapping and functional annotation were re-mapped using the update files from GenBank Friedman et al. 2018.
  • Candida parapsilosis CDC 317 – The genome was reloaded to include the mitochondrial genome Butler et al. 2009.
  • Neurospora crassa OR74A – The genome was reloaded. Incorrect gene aliases reflecting The Broad Institution v4 (June 2010) gene set were previously loaded to FungiDB due to two dates of issue (February and June). This was rectified with remapping of these aliases using only the February version gene ids. Galagan et al. 2009.

Transcriptomics

  • Transcriptome profiling of Candida auris clinical isolates forming biofilm – Transcriptomic investigation of isolates collected by Public Health England, UK using Illumina HiSeq X Ten technology. Kean et al. 2018.
  • Transcriptome analysis of Aspergillus fumigatus grown in blood – Gene expression profile at various stages of an in vitro model of aspergillosis. Irmer et al. 2015.
  • Transcriptomes of itraconazole-resistant strains derived from A1160, HapB and 29.9. – RNA-Seq of Aspergillus fumigatus mutants resistant to itraconazole.
  • Stranded RNA-Seq of WT and cqs1delta strains during sexual development stage – Stranded RNA-Seq of C. neoformans JEC21 WT (XL280α) and cqs1Δ strains grown on V8 media. Tian et al. 2018.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans sexual reproduction is controlled by a quorum sensing peptide – Transcriptomic analysis of gene expression in Cryptococcus neoformans var neoformans targeting pheromone pathways and quorum sensing. Tian et al. 2018.
  • Transcriptomics of banana pathogen F. oxysporum f.sp. cubense race 4 – Strand specific RNA-Seq analysis of Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. cubense race 4 infecting Musa acuminata at 0h and 48h. Guo et al. 2014.
  • Transcriptome of heat-stressed Fusarium graminearum Z-3639 – Illumina HiSeq 2000 of 24 h-old mycelia from complete medium incubated in 25C and 37C for 15 min. Bui et al. 2016.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - Candida auris isolates – Multidrug-resistant Candida auris isolates were collected from on 3 continents and sequenced Illumina HiSeq 2500 technology. Lockhart et al. 2017.
  • SNP calls on Paracoccidioides lutzii WGS – Whole genome sequencing of P. lutzii isolates. Muñoz et al. 2018.

Phenotype data

  • Manually Curated Fusarium Phenotypes – The phenotypes for Fusarium graminearum PH-1 are manually curated from published literature describing phenotypes obtained from mutant studies. We have curated and added phenotypes from Son et al. 2011 publication on transcription factors to the currently existing Fusarium phenotypes from Yun et al. 2007. We followed an "entity + quality" approach and curated phenotypes with multiple ontologies: Phenotypic Quality Ontology (PATO) for "quality" terms and Gene Ontology (GO), Fungal Gross Anatomy Ontology (FAO), Chemical Entities of Biological Interest (ChEBI) and Ascomycete Phenotype Ontology (APO) for "entity" terms.

New features in FungiDB 42

  • Updated Alignment Tool on Gene Pages –
    The Clustal Omega alignment tool in the 'Orthologs and Paralogs within EuPathDB' table now accommodates protein, CDS and genomic sequence alignments. To run Clustal Omega, select genes from the table. Then choose the sequence type and initiate the alignment with the 'Run Clutal Omega for selected genes' button. Output formats include Mismatches highlighted, FASTA, PHYLIP, STOCKHOLM, and VIENNA. This tool replaces the 'Retrieve multiple sequence alignment or multi-FASTA' tool.
  • Beta access to JBrowse –
    A beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser, is available from gene pages. Use the JBrowse Genome Browser link under the graphic Shortcuts on the gene page. Example Link Location
  • New Gene Page STRING tool –
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence to the STRING functional protein association network. Szklarczyk et al.
  • Result Table Improvements –
    Result column configurations and sorting are now saved for each strategy step and will be retained when you come back or when you share a strategy with someone else.
  • New Options for FASTA downloads –
    The header line of FASTA formatted downloads can be configured to contain only the gene ID. The sequence can be configured to appear on a signle line or the standard 60 characters per line.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
