New data in FungiDB 48

Transcriptomics

  • Aspergillus fumigatus : Transcriptomes of WT, ΔnctA, and ΔnctB mutants in response to itraconazole. – Genome-wide gene expression profile was investigated in the wild-type and null mutants. Furukawa et al. 2019.
  • Neurospora crassa : Transcriptome changes in response to genotoxic stress. – Transcriptomes of WT and H3K9me3-deficient mutants. Basenko et al. 2015.
  • Fusarium graminearum : Transcriptome in response to an active or passive host. – Transcriptomes of cells growing on live and dead wheat heads. Boedi et al. 2016.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans : Transcriptome and translatome changes in response to host temperature stress. – Total and polysome-associated mRNA profiles of two strains during optimal conditions and one hour following host temperature stress. Bloom et al. 2019.

Chip-Seq data

  • Genome-wide binding profiles of C-terminally S-tagged NctA protein. – Genome-wide binding profiles of NctA protein in the absence and the presence of itraconazole. Furukawa et al. 2019.

Phenotype data

  • Manually curated Cryptococcus Phenotypes – The phenotypes for Cryptococcus neoformans JEC21 and H99 and Cryptococcus gatii WM276 are manually curated from published literature describing phenotypes obtained from mutant studies. We have curated and added phenotypes from Bairwa et al. 2019. publication on kinases to the currently existing phenotypic dataset for C. neoformans H99. We followed an "entity + quality" approach and curated phenotypes with multiple ontologies: Phenotypic Quality Ontology (PATO) for "quality" terms and Gene Ontology (GO), Fungal Gross Anatomy Ontology (FAO), Chemical Entities of Biological Interest (ChEBI) and Ascomycete Phenotype Ontology (APO) for "entity" terms.
