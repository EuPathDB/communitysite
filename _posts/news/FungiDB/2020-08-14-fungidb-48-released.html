New data in FungiDB 48

Transcriptomics

  • Aspergillus fumigatus : Transcriptomes of WT, ΔnctA, and ΔnctB mutants in response to itraconazole – Genome-wide gene expression profile was investigated in the wild-type and null mutants. Furukawa et al. 2019.
  • Neurospora crassa : Transcriptome changes in response to genotoxic stress – Transcriptomes of WT and H3K9me3-deficient mutants. Basenko et al. 2015.
  • Fusarium graminearum : Transcriptome in response to an active or passive host – Transcriptomes of cells growing on live and dead wheat heads. Boedi et al. 2016.
  • Cryptococcus neoformans : Transcriptome and translatome changes in response to host temperature stress – Total and polysome-associated mRNA profiles of two strains during optimal conditions and one hour following host temperature stress. Bloom et al. 2019.

Chip-Seq data

  • Aspergillus fumigatus : Genome-wide binding profiles of C-terminally S-tagged NctA protein – Genome-wide binding profiles of NctA protein in the absence and the presence of itraconazole. Furukawa et al. 2019.

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Aspergillus fumigatus : SNP calls on A. fumigatus WGS – Thirteen clinical isogenic isolates were sequenced using Illumina NextSeq 500 technology. Ballard et al. 2019.

Phenotype data

  • Aspergillus fumigatus : Images from the COFUN Aspergillus fumigatus transcription factor KO collection were loaded on gene record pages for Aspergillus fumigatus Af293 and Aspergillus fumigatus A1163 – Part of the COFUN gene‐wide knock out collection, consisting of 392 transcription factors knock out mutants Furukawa et al. 2019.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
