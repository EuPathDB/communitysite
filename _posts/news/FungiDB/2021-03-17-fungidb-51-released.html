    Phenotype data

    • Manually Curated Aspergillus Phenotypes – The currently existing phenotypes for Aspergillus species were manually curated by AspGD from published literature describing phenotypes obtained from mutant studies. These phenotypic annotations were reformatted at FungiDB in-line with VEuPathDB’s approach for phenotype curation. We have curated and added phenotypes from additional publications to the currently existing dataset for Aspergillus fumigatus Af293 and Aspergillus nidulans FGSC A4 in this release.

    • Manually Curated Cryptococcus Phenotypes – The phenotypes for Cryptococcus neoformans JEC21 and H99 and Cryptococcus gattii WM276 genomes are manually curated from published literature describing phenotypes obtained from mutant studies. We have curated and added phenotypes from additional publications to the currently existing phenotypic dataset for C. neoformans H99 in this release. These curated publications include large-scale knockout collections from Lee et al. 2020 and Jin et al. 2020 among several others.

    Species Phenotype mapping Genes with phenotypes
    Cryptococcus neoformans var grubii H99 4891 534

    • Manually Curated Magnaporthe Phenotypes – The phenotypes for Magnaporthe oryzae 70-15 are manually curated from published literature describing phenotypes obtained from mutant studies. These phenotypes have been manually curated in-line with VEuPathDB's approach for phenotype annotation and integrated into FungiDB. We followed an "entity + quality" approach and curated phenotypes with multiple ontologies: Phenotypic Quality Ontology (PATO) for "quality" terms and Gene Ontology (GO), Fungal Gross Anatomy Ontology (FAO), Chemical Entities of Biological Interest (ChEBI) and Ascomycete Phenotype Ontology (APO) for "entity" terms. We have curated and integrated phenotypes for bZIP and Zn2Cys6 transcription factors from four publications in this release.

New features in {{project}} 50

  • OrthoMCL DB now shares web architecture with other VEuPathDB sites. The updated and streamlined interface emphasizes easy access to tools and help information.
    • OrthoMCL now includes orthology information for 564 species, including all of the VEuPathDB organisms with annotated genomes.
    • The redesigned home page includes a new site-wide search for easily finding groups, proteins or documents. A categorized list of Searches appears on the left of the home page and the center section is devoted to tutorials for getting you started with OrthoMCL features.
    • Group and protein record pages are redesigned and include a contents section for navigating to the data you are interested in.
    • For more information see the OrthoMCL DB About page, the FAQ page or complete this tutorial.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
