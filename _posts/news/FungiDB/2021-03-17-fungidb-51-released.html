New data in FungiDB 50

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • Coccidioides posadasii : SNP calls on WGS of Coccidioides posadasii isolates from regions bordering the Caribbean Sea. – Ten isolates were sequenced using Illumina HiSeq 2500 technology. Teixeira et al. 2019.
  • Coccidioides immitis : SNP calls on WGS of Coccidioides immitis isolates from Washington State, USA – Fourteen clinical isolates were sequenced using Illumina HiSeq 2500 technology. Oltean et al. 2019.

Transcriptomics

  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida albicans — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018. The host component of this data set is available on HostDB: Fresh human whole blood infected with Candida albicans.
  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida parapsilosis — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018. The fungal component of this data set is available on FungiDB: Fresh human whole blood infected with Candida parapsilosis.
  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida tropicalis — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018. The fungal component of this data set is available on FungiDB: Fresh human whole blood infected with Candida tropicalis.
  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida glabrata — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018. The fungal component of this data set is available on FungiDB: Fresh human whole blood infected with Candida glabrata.
  • Early RNA sequencing of human monocytes in presence of Candida albicans — RNA sequencing was prerformed from Candida albicans co-incubated monoyctes from 3 different donors. Halder et al. 2020.
  • Transcriptional landscape of Cryptococcus - host response — Animal infection model using the mouse Mus musculus to study the host response to infection. Li al. 2019.
  • Mouse macrophages infected with Mucor circinelloides — A study of the transcriptomics changes occuring in both host and pathogen upon the interaction of Mucor circinelloides spores with phagocytic cells of a mouse. Pérez-Arques al. 2019.

Proteomics

  • Aspergillus clavatus : Proteome changes in response to human serum – Activity-based protein profiling was used to identify serum-responsive proteins over time (24 and 48 hrs). Wiedner et al. 2013.
  • Aspergillus fischeri : Proteome changes in response to human serum – Activity-based protein profiling was used to identify serum-responsive proteins over time (24 and 48 hrs). Wiedner et al. 2013.
  • Aspergillus fumigatus : Proteome changes in response to human serum – Activity-based protein profiling was used to identify serum-responsive proteins over time (24 and 48 hrs). Wiedner et al. 2013.

New features in {{project}} 50

  • OrthoMCL DB now shares web architecture with other VEuPathDB sites. The updated and streamlined interface emphasizes easy access to tools and help information.
    • OrthoMCL now includes orthology information for 564 species, including all of the VEuPathDB organisms with annotated genomes.
    • The redesigned home page includes a new site-wide search for easily finding groups, proteins or documents. A categorized list of Searches appears on the left of the home page and the center section is devoted to tutorials for getting you started with OrthoMCL features.
    • Group and protein record pages are redesigned and include a contents section for navigating to the data you are interested in.
    • For more information see the OrthoMCL DB About page, the FAQ page or complete this tutorial.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
