Genome Sequence

Whole genome sequence of isolates for SNP analysis:

  • SNP calls on WGS of clinical isolates of A. lentulus – Aspergillus lentulus IFM54703 is a causative agent of aspergillosis. Six clinical isolates were sequenced. Dos Santos et al. 2020.
  • SNP calls on WGS of Candida haemulonii isolates – Isolates were received in the Mycotic Diseases Branch Reference Laboratory at the CDC for routine fungal identification or as part of ongoing fungal disease surveillance. Gade et al. 2020.
  • SNP calls on WGS of Pyricularia field-isolates – Whole-genome analysis of fungal strains from different hosts. Gladieux et al. 2018.

Transcriptomics

  • Comparative transcriptome of Candida parapsilosis wild type and transcription factor mutants — Transcriptomes collected from WT in planktonic growth conditions and WT and seven transcription factor mutants grown in biofilm conditions. Holland et al. 2014.
  • Transcriptomics of Mucor circinelloides NCRIP deficient spores phagocytosed by mouse macrophages — Transcriptomics of R3B2 and RDRP1 fungal spores and fungal spore-macrophage coculture. Pérez-Arques et al. 2020.

Phenotype data

  • Manually Curated Aspergillus Phenotypes – The currently existing phenotypes for Aspergillus species were manually curated by AspGD from published literature describing phenotypes obtained from mutant studies. These phenotypic annotations were reformatted at FungiDB in-line with VEuPathDB’s approach for phenotype curation.
    • Aspergillus fumigatus Af293: We have curated and integrated phenotypes from Thammahong et al. 2019. Phenotype Mapping: 27. Genes with phenotypes: 1.
    • Cryptococcus neoformans H99: We have curated and integrated phenotypes from Thammahong et al. 2019. Phenotype Mapping: 100. Genes with phenotypes: 27.
    • Fusrium graminearum PH-1: We have curated and integrated phenotypes from Thammahong et al. 2019. Phenotype Mapping: 99. Genes with phenotypes: 50.

Updated Functional Annotation

  • Summary of annotation updates which include product descriptions and notes from curators. These functional annotations are current with GeneDB April 2021 GFF versions.
    • Aspergillus fumigatus Af293: GeneDB GFF file version April, 2021. Genes updated: 240.

New features in {{project}} 52

  • BLASTbeta tool – a new BLAST tool with multi-BLAST capabilities. Input a single sequence or several FASTA-formatted sequences at once. Our tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. BLASTbeta video introduction (4 min)
  • Usage and performance metrics for VEuPathDB sites are now updated monthly.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
