We are pleased to announce the release of GiardiaDB 4.0

New data in this release:
  • Giardia subcellular localization data: This data was funded by the EuPathDB driving biological project mechanism and constitutes the first batch of subcellular localization data.  This data has been provided prepublication by Dr. Scott Dawson.  View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section.  
  • Genome sequence and annotation from Giardia Assemblages A2 isolate DH and B isolate GS (Adam et al.): View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section. 

  • This release of GiardiaDB constitutes a complete rebuild of the database. The latest versions of genome sequence and annotation were loaded and all analyses were rerun and updated (InterProSCan, SignalP, functional data mapping, etc.).

 New Tutorials:

Learn how to upload custom tracks in the genome browser. 

Our YouTube channel is updated regularly with new tutorials.  The latest tutorials include:
  • Transform by Orthology Across Taxon Groups 
  • Transform by Orthology within a Taxon Group
  • Orthology information on gene pages
  • Inferring UTR length using RNA sequencing data
  • Decreasing Gene Page Load Time

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary.  Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
