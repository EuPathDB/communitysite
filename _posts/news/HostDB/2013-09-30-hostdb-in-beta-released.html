We are pleased to announce a beta release of HostDB


HostDB is the newest addition to the Eukaryotic Pathogen databases (EuPathDB).  Apply the powerful search strategy system to query functional data from host organisms during infection with parasites.  The beta release includes the following datasets:


  • The transcriptomes of primary murine macrophages (M. musculus C57BL6J) during infection with 25 strains of Toxoplasma gondii (Minot et al.).  For full attribution and available searches please visit the Dataset details. 

  • Proteomic analysis of Homo Sapiens during infection with 4 Toxoplasma gondii (GT1, ME49, RH and VEG) strains graciously provided prepublication by the Johnathan Wastling group (University of Liverpool).  For full attribution and available searches please visit the Dataset details. This data was generated as part of one of the EuPathDB-funded driving biological projects.

We look forward to expanding this database in data content and functionality.  We encourage you to explore HostDB and provide us with your feedback.
