We are pleased to announce the release of HostDB 26

New Features

GBrowse Track Search

  • The Select Tracks page in the Genome Browser now includes a search function. Search for specific tracks based on a word or phrase from the track title or category. image

New Tutorials

  • Find genes with an enriched GO term
  • Downloading untranslated regions of genes
  • Download sequence for genes in a search result
  • Downloading sequence from a gene page
  • Download the sequence of all genes for an organism
  • Download the Sequence (fasta) and Annotation (gff) files for a genome
  • Downloading coding sequence
  • Downloading protein sequence


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
