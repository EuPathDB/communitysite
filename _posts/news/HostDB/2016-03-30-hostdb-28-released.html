We are pleased to announce the release of HostDB 28

Updated Data sets

Proteomics

The following data sets were reloaded at the request of the data provider (J. Wastling) because the data analysis was significantly improved by applying multiple search engines. The new analysis produced more peptide/protein identifications and more reliable quantitation.
  • Quantitative Proteome During an Infection with 4 strains of T. gondii and one strain of N. caninum — H. sapiens peptide sequences from a proteomic analysis of mixed parasite/host samples collected during time course infections with 4 different strains of T. gondii. Wastling and Xia, unpublished
  • Proteome During Infection with 4 strains of T. gondii and one strain of N. caninum — H. sapiens peptide sequences from a proteomic analysis of a mixed parasite/host sample collected during T. gondii infections in human foreskin fibroblasts. Wastling and Xia, unpublished


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
