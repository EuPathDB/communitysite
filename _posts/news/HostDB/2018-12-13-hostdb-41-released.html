We are pleased to announce the release of HostDB 41

New data in HostDB 41

RNA Sequence

  • HFF transcriptional response to virulent and avirulent T. cruzi —
    Transcriptional response of human foreskin fibroblasts following infection with T. cruzi. RNA was sequenced from infected HFF at 60 and 96 hours post infection and from uninfected controls. Belew et al. This data set has T. cruzi components available in TriTrypDB as:
    Comparative transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi (Esmo aligned)
    Comparative transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi (Non-Esmo aligned)
    Comparative transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi (CLBrener).

New features in HostDB 41

Introducing Clustal Omega


EuPathDB sites are now using Clustal Omega for gene page protein alignments in the 'Orthologs and Paralogs within EuPathDB' table and the 'Retrieve multiple sequence alignment or multi-FASTA' tool in the 'Orthology and synteny' section of gene pages. The 'Isolate Alignments in this Gene Region' tool still uses CulstalW.

New Gene page tools

  • Interpro —
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence for sequence analysis and classification via InterPro.
  • GPI anchor prediction —
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence for GPI modification site prediction via the GPI Lipid Anchor Project.
