New data in HostDB 43

RNA Sequence

  • Transcriptome of Bos taurus during infection with virulent and avirulent N. caninum strains — Transcriptomic analysis of Bos taurus during infection with two different isolates of Neospora caninum, the highly virulent isolate Nc-Spain7 and the comparatively avirulent isolate Nc-Spain1H. Horcajo et al. 2017 and Horcajo et al. 2018
    This data was also aligned to the N. caninum genome and the parasite data is available in ToxoDB in the following data sets
    Parasite trascriptome during infection in Bos taurus cells
    Purified parasite transcriptome of Nc-Spain strains
  • Transcriptome of mouse bone marrow derived macrophages infected by wild-type and gra18 mutant strains of T. gondii — The raw sequence reads were also aligned to the T. gondii genome and is available in ToxoDB.
    Transcriptome of wild-type and gra18 mutant strains of T. gondii
    He et al. 2017

New features in HostDB 43

  • A beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser is now available from gene record pages on any EuPathDB site.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
