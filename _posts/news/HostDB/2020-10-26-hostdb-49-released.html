New data in HostDB 49

Transcriptomics

  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida albicans — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018.

  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida parapsilosis — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018.

  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida tropicalis — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018.

  • Fresh whole blood infected with Candida glabrata — RNA-pool of both species extracted at 0min (control), 15, 30, 60, 120 and 240 min. Sieber et al. 2018.

  • Early RNA sequencing of human monocytes in presence of Candida albicans — RNA sequencing was prerformed from Candida albicans co-incubated monoyctes from 3 different donors. Halder et al. 2020.

  • Transcriptional landscape of Cryptococcus - host response — Animal infection model using the non-human primate cynomolgus monkey Macaca fascicularis and Mus musculus to study host response to infection. Li al. 2019.

  • Mouse macrophages infected with Mucor circinelloides — A study of the transcriptomics changes occuring in both host and pathogen upon the interaction of Mucor circinelloides spores with phagocytic cells of a mouse. Pérez-Arques al. 2019.



Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
