New data in HostDB 51

Tanscriptomics

  • PBMCs stimulated with live Candida spp. and their cell wall components – RNA sequencing profiles of peripheral blood mononuclear cells stimulated for 4h and 24h with live C. albicans and C. auris and their cell wall components. Bruno et al. 2020.

New features in {{project}} 51

  • BLASTbeta tool – a new BLAST tool with multi-BLAST capabilities. Input a single sequence or several fasta-formatted sequences at once. Our tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence.
  • Usage and performance metrics for VEuPathDB sites are now updated monthly.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.
