New data in HostDB 52

EBI Pipeline

{{project}} 52 represents the first release that employs the EBI pipeline for analyzing genomes and RNA-Seq data. ({{project}} Analysis Methods) All genomes were run through the EBI pipeline, updating all analyses such as InterproScan and repeat identification. While much of the EBI pipeline is the same as our previous in-house pipeline, these differences are noted:
  • Expression values calculated from RNA-Seq data are now presented as TPM (Transcripts Per Kilobase Million) instead of FPKM (Fragments Per Kilobase Million) or RPKM (Reads Per Kilobase Million). Learn more
  • Signal peptide predictions are now made with SignalP-4.1, updated from SignalP-3.0.
  • All pseudogenes now include automatically predicted UTRs.

Annotated Genome

  • Macaca fascicularis REF – This annotated genome appears for the first time in HostDB. Li et al. 2019.

Transcriptomics

  • Host response to early and mid-stage P. berghei liver infection – Transcriptome sequencing (RNA-Seq) analysis throughout the Plasmodium berghei liver stage, covering as early as 2 hrs postinfection (hpi) and extending to 48 hpi in HuH7 and HepG2 cells. Toro-Moreno et al. 2020.
    • Associated PlasmoDB data: Transcriptome during early and mid-stage P. berghei liver infection
  • Transcriptional landscape of Cryptococcus-host response – Animal infection model using the non-human primate cynomolgus monkey Macaca fascicularis to study host response to infection. Li et al. 2019.
    • Associated HostDB data: Transcriptional landscape of Cryptococcus-host response, Mus musculus study
    • Associated FungiDB data: Transcriptional landscape of Cryptococcus-host response, Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii H99 infection in Mus musculus and Macaca fascicularis

New features in {{project}} 52

  • BLASTbeta tool – a new BLAST tool with multi-BLAST capabilities. Input a single sequence or several FASTA-formatted sequences at once. Our tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. BLASTbeta video introduction (4 min)
  • Usage and performance metrics for VEuPathDB sites are now updated monthly.
