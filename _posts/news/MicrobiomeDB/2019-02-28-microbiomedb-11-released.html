We are pleased to announce the release of MicrobiomeDB 11

New data in this release

  • The ENTiCE study includes stool, rectal swab, and GI biopsy microbiome data from 42 dogs undergoing treatment for Canine Chronic Enteropathy.
  • The CAMP study is a case control design that includes stool microbiome data from 155 dogs with confirmed enteric parasite infections, and 115 healthy dogs.
  • The Experimental Cutaneous Leishmaniasis study is a longitudinal study in which stool and skin swabs were collected from mice experimentally infected with Leishmania major.
