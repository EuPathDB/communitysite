We are pleased to announce the release of MicrobiomeDB 18

New data in this release

  • The Broad Institute's DIABIMMUNE study of three pediatric cohorts that examined the microbiome in > 100 children from birth to ~ 3yr old who are at high risk for development of Type 1 diabetes (T1D).
  • The PIH-Uganda study, which profiled the microbial community in cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) samples collected from ~100 children from Uganda, that presented with either postinfectious hydrocephalus (PIH) or non-postinfectious hydrocephalus (NPIH).

New features in this release

  • We are excited to announce a new feature that allows users to directly upload their own .biom file (created by popular 16S pipelines, such as Mothur and QIIME) for analysis using microbiomeDB filter tools and data visualization apps. All uploaded .biom data are stored in a private 'User Data Sets' page accessbile under the 'Workspace' menu on the MicrobbiomeDB homepage.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
