--- layout: post title: OrthoMCL 6.3 released category: [OrthoMCL,VEuPathDB] --- OrthoMCL Version 6.3 is released. In this release, 1 Peripheral species was added. Thus, OrthoMCL now predicts orthology for a total of 564 organisms (414 Peripheral and 150 Core organisms). Proteins from the 1 new species were mapped into Core ortholog groups. Then, a new set of Residual ortholog groups (e.g. OG6r3_101799) were formed from the collection of all unmapped Peripheral proteins. See the About page for the methods.
