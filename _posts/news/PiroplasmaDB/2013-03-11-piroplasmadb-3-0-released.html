We are pleased to announce the release of PiropasmaDB 3.0

New data in this release:


Genomic sequence and annotation for Babesia microti R1


The Babesia microti R1 genome and annotation (Genbank) is fully integrated into PiroplasmaDB (Cornillot et al.).  Use the searches available in PiroplasmaDB to find B. microti genes or genomic sequences  based on a variety of parameters and combine searches to identify genes with specific characteristics.  For example, the following strategy combines several searches to find signal peptide and/or transmembrane domain containing genes that are conserved in Piroplasma but not in Haemosporidia.


 http://piroplasmadb.org/piro/im.do?s=7c44f6999344e543


New Functionality:

PCR Primer Design tool added to GBrowse.

Design PCR primers to any region of the genome using this interactive, graphic interface. To reach this tool, open the PiroplasmaDB Genome Browser at your genomic region of interest, choose "Design PCR primers" from the dropdown menu and click "GO".  To design primers, focus on a PCR product size range by highlighting the area on the scale, then enter your preferred PCR parameters in the text boxes and click Design Primer.

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary.  Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
