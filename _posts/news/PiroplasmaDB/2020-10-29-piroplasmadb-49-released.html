New data in {{project}} 49

Genome sequence and annotation

  • Babesia sp. Xinjiang strain Xinjiang – Sequence and annotated the 8.4 Mb nuclear draft genome of Babesia sp. Xinjiang strain Xinjiang. Guan et al. 2016

Unannotated genome sequence

  • Babesia divergens strain Rouen 1987 – Babesia divergens genome assembly was improved by exploiting previous sequence data and new computational tools and assembly strategies. Gonzalez et al. 2019
  • Babesia microti strain GreenwichYale_Lab_strain_1 – Sequenced with Illumina methods and assembled with MaSuRCA v. 1.9. From the laboratory of Joana Carneiro da Silva.
  • Babesia microti strain GI – Sequenced with Illumina methods and assembled with Celera Assembler v. 7.0. From the laboratory of Joana Carneiro da Silva.
  • Babesia microti strain ATCC 30222 – Sequenced with Illumina methods and assembled with Celera Assembler v. 7.0. From the laboratory of Joana Carneiro da Silva.
  • Babesia microti strain Naushon – Sequenced with Illumina methods and assembled with Celera Assembler v. 7.0. From the laboratory of Joana Carneiro da Silva.
  • Babesia microti strain Nan_Hs_2011_N11-50 – Sequenced with Illumina methods and assembled with Celera Assembler v. 7.0. From the laboratory of Joana Carneiro da Silva.
  • Babesia microti strain ATCC PRA-99 – Sequenced with Illumina methods and assembled with Celera Assembler v. 7.0. From the laboratory of Joana Carneiro da Silva.

Transcriptomics

  • Merozoite transcriptome – Transcriptome of the merozoite stage for Babesia sp Xinjiang determined by Illumina RNA-sequencing. Guan et al. 2016

New features in {{project}} 49

  • Gene pages: The sequence section now contains links to copy the sequence to your clipboard. Sequences are copied in FASTA format with the gene ID in the def line.
  • Gene pages: It is now possible to scroll and zoom within the JBrowse views that are present in the 'Gene models' and 'Protein features and properties' sections.
  • Contact Us form: A new option on the Contact Us form makes it possible to paste a screenshot into the form when sending us questions or suggestions.
  • Search result page: The Download and Add to Basket options from a search result tab now appear as buttons for better visibility.
  • Error messages: The text in our error messages was reviewed and edited to improve clarity. And we've made it easier to report errors by adding links in error messages to the Contact Us form.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
