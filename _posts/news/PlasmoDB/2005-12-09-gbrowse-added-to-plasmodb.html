--- layout: post title: GBrowse added to PlasmoDB category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,PlasmoDB] --- GBrowse is an interactive generic genome browser developed as part of the GMOD project (www.gmod.org). GBrowse is used in PlasmoDB to display gene models, EST alignments, and synteny relationships between Plasmodium species. With GBrowse, we now provide:
  • Custom restriction site identification
  • Open reading frame identification
  • Custom downloads in various formats:
    • FASTA
    • GFF (2)
    • GFF3
    • BSML (XML)
    • EMBL
    • GenBank
    • GAME
    • GCG
