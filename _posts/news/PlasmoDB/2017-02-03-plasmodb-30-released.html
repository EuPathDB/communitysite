We are pleased to announce the release of PlasmoDB 30

New Data in Release 30

Genome Sequence and Annotation:

  • Plasmodium coatneyi strain Hackeri – Updated to the re-sequenced and re-annotated GenBank version from the MaHPIC Consortium. Chien et al.
  • Plasmodium fragile strain nilgiri – New to PlasmoDB and integrated as the Genbank version from the Broad Institute Plasmodium 100 Genomes initiative. (unpublished).
  • Plasmodium inui strain San Antonio 1 – Sequenced at the Broad Institute. New to PlasmoDB and integrated as the Genbank version Jan 31, 2014.
  • Plasmodium malariae strain UG01 – First release of this genome in PlasmoDB (version from 19th of September 2016). Provided by the Parasite Genomics Group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.
  • Plasmodium ovale strain curtisi GH01 – First release of this genome in PlasmoDB (version from 19th of September 2016). Provided by the Parasite Genomics Group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.
  • Plasmodium vinckei petteri strain CR – Sequenced at the Broad Institute. New to PlasmoDB and integrated as the Genbank version 27 Jan 2014.
  • Plasmodium vinckei vinckei strain vinckei – Sequenced at the Broad Institute. New to PlasmoDB and integrated as the Genbank version 17 June 2014.
  • Plasmodium yoelii yoelii 17X – The genome was updated to the reassembled and reannotated GeneDB version 28 Oct 2016. Provided by the Parasite Genomics Group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute. This is a completed genome (version 3), in 14 pieces with no gaps.

Proteomics

  • Male and Female Gametocyte Proteomes – Proteomics data for male, female and mixed P. falciparum 3D7 gametocytes. This transcriptomics data set has a corresponding proteomics data set called Female and Male Gametocyte Transcriptomes. Lasonder et al.
  • Sporozoite surface proteome – Proteome of P. falciparum NF54 salivary gland sporozoite surface proteins isolated by affinity enrichment of surface-exposed proteins labeled with a biotin tag. Swearingen et al.

RNA Sequencing

  • Female and Male Gametocyte Transcriptomes – Directional RNA-seq data from P. falciparum. This transcriptomics data set has a corresponding proteomics data set called Male and Female Gametocyte Proteomes. Lasonder et al.
  • Transcription profile of intraerythrocytic cycle – Transcriptomes of the P. vivax intraerythrocytic cycle from two clinical isolates collected from Northwestern Thailand. Zhu et al.

Whole genome sequencing of isolates

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - Field and monkey adapted isolates – Whole genome sequencing of five P. vivax isolates obtained from Malagasy and Cambodian patients, and of the monkey-adapted Belem strain. Chan et al.
  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - strain SY57 – Whole genome sequencing of Plasmodium reichenowi SY57. The data were used to call SNPs against the Plasmodium reichenowi CDC reference genome. Sundararaman et al.

Phenotypes

  • RMgmDB - Rodent Malaria genetically modified Parasites – This data set is updated to the RMgm version from 07 Nov 2016. Mutant phenotypes are described in the Phenotype table of corresponding gene pages for P. berhei ANKA, P. falciparum 3D7, and P. yoelii YM.
