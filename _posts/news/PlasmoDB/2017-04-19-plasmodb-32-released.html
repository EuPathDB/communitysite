We are pleased to announce the release of PlasmoDB 32

New Data in Release 32

Genome Sequence and Annotation:

  • P. gallinaceum 8A – first release of this genome in PlasmoDB (version from 9th of January 2017). Provided by the Parasite Genomics Group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.
  • Plasmodium relictum SGS1 – first release of this genome in PlasmoDB (version from 9th of January 2017). Provided by the Parasite Genomics Group at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.
