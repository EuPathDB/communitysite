We are pleased to announce the release of PlasmoDB 38

Announcement:

Starting with PlasmoDB 38, P. vivax P01 replaces P. vivax Sal-1 as reference for all P. vivax data in PlasmoDB. Functional genomics data for all strains of P. vivax are now aligned to P. vivax P01. PlasmoDB 37, which uses P. vivax Sal-1 as reference is temporarily available here: http://r37.plasmodb.org/plasmo.b37/

New data in PlasmoDB 38

RNA Sequence

  • Hypnozoite RNAseq – P. vivax hypnozoite RNAseq Gural et al.
  • Ring, Oocyst and Sporozoite Transcriptomes – Transcriptomes of oocyst, ring and sporozoite stages in P. falciparum determined by RNA sequence. Zanghi et al.

ChIP Sequencing data

  • Epigenome of sporozoites (HP-1, H3K9me3, H3K9ac) and rings (HP-1, H3K9me3) – Genome-wide distribution of HP-1 and H3K9me3 in P. falciparum rings and sporozoites, as well as H3K9ac in sporozoites. Zanghi et al.

No new features in PlasmoDB 38
