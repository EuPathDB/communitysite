New data in PlasmoDB 43

RNA Sequence

  • Strand specific transcriptome of the intraerythrocytic developmental cycle – Strand specific RNA seq analysis of intraerythrocytic cycle (10, 20, 30, 40 hpi). Siegel et al.
  • Hypnozoite, schizont and blood stage transcriptomes (laser microdissection) – Plasmodium cynomolgi hypnozoites and schizonts were isolated by laser capture microdissection from infected monkey hepatocytes. These samples were analyzed by RNA seq along with RNA extracted from frozen, infected M. fascicularis blood samples. Cubi et al.

ChIP-Seq

  • H3K4me3, H3K9me3, HP1, H4K31me1 & H4K31ac histone post-translational modifications – Sindikubwabo et al.
