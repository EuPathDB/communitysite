New data in PlasmoDB 44

RNA Sequence

  • Intraerythrocytic development cycle transcriptome (2018) – Strand specific RNA-seq analysis of 8 time points during the P. falciparum 3D7 intraerythrocytic development cycle. Toenhake et al.
  • Sporozoite transcriptome in different microenvironments – Plasmodium vivax transcriptome in 4 microenvironments at 3 time points and 2 temperatures to investigate th e skin-to-liver-stage journey. Roth et al.

ChIP-Seq

  • Regulatory elements during intraerythrocytic development by ATACseq – Chromatin accessibility defined by directly profiling chromatin accessibility using the assay for transposase accessible chromatin sequencing (ATAC-seq) at eight time points during the intraerythrocytic cycle. Toenhake et al.

New features in PlasmoDB 44

  • Synteny and the Protein Browser are availabe in our beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser.
  • New EuPathDB RNA Seq Tool. The EuPathDB Galaxy Workspace now offers a tool that converts read counts from HTSeq-count output to FPKM values.
  • New EuPathDB Workflows. The EuPathDB Galaxy Workspace is updated with several new pre-configured workflows that are designed to allow export of strand specific data to EuPathDB for visualization and searching.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
