New data in PlasmoDB 45

RNA Sequence

  • Asexual blood stages, salivary gland sporozoite and midgut oocyst transcriptomes – Transcriptional analysis of asexual blood stages from 4 infected donors and the salivary gland sporozoites and midgut oocysts resulting from infecting mosquitoes with their blood. Gomez-Diaz et al.

Microarray

  • Gametocyte time course from commitment to maturity – Comprehensive high-resolution transcriptome dataset spanning the whole of gametocyte commitment and development of P. falciparum. BioRxiv, Biljon et al.

New features in PlasmoDB 45

  • All EuPathDB sites now offer the JBrowse Genome Browser. GBrowse will be phased out in coming releases. Please Contact Us with comments and suggestions for the genome browser.
  • The new RNASeq Transcript Summary tool on gene pages helps reveal experiments in which the gene is highly regulated. The tool displays the gene's FPKM values for all experiments in PlasmoDB and offers features such as log/linear scaling, zoom and pan to explore the gene's expression in each experiment.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
