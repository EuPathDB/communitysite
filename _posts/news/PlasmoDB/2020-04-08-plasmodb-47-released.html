We are pleased to announce the release of PlasmoDB 47.

The product of 6 months of back-end infrastructure work, Release 47 includes beta.plasmodb.org in addition to our traditional sites. The beta site contains the same data, tools and functions in an updated and streamlined interface that emphasizes easy access to tools and help information.

Your feedback is invaluable as we improve our sites and transtition to our new interface. So please try beta.plasmodb.org and let us know how we are doing by completing one or all of the surveys below. Each takes about half an hour to complete.

  • Site search survey
