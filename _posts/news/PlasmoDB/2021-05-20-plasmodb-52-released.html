New data in {{project}} 52

Genome Sequence

  • Haemoproteus tartakovskyi strain SISKIN1 – Bensch et al. 2016.

Transcriptomics

  • Ex-erythrocytic stage transcriptomes (sporozoite, liver time course and detached cells) – RNA-seq analyses of various exo-erythrocytic stages of Plasmodium berghei including sporozoites, a time-course of liver stage development and detached cells. Caldelari et al. 2019.
  • Transcriptome of the asexual life stages – RNA sequence analysis of ring, trophozoite and schizont stages as biological duplicates. Tang et al. 2020.

Proteomics

  • PfCRK4 regulated phosphoproteome at 29 and 37 hpi – Phosphoproteome of D10-PfCRK4-HA-DD parasites at 29 and 37 hours hours post infection. Peptides associated with proteins found in the phosphoproteome were mapped to the P. falciparum 3D7 genome. Ganter et al. 2017
  • PfCRK4 regulated proteome at 29 and 37 hpi (quantitative) – Proteome determined at 29- and 37- hour post infection in Plasmodium falciparum using a clone in which PfCRK4 was conditionally destabilized. (+Shield = stable PfCRK4, -Shield = destabilized PfCRK4)Ganter et al. 2017
  • Proteome of ApiAP2 double vs single knockout – Proteome deregulation in the purified schizonts of ap2-g/ap2-g2 double knockout compared to the parental ap2-g mutant clone.Modrzynska et al. 2017

Genome Browser Tracks

GFF files showing UTRs for Pf3D7, PfIT, and PfHB3 strains determined by DAFT- and 5UTR-Seq RNA sequencing techniques. Data are shown aligned to the 3D7 genome. Chappell et al. 2020.
  • UTRs based on DAFT- and UTR-Seq (3D7 2020)
  • UTRs based on DAFT- and UTR-Seq (HB3 2020)
  • UTRs based on DAFT- and UTR-Seq (IT 2020)
  • JBrowse view of all three tracks

New features in {{project}} 52

  • BLASTbeta tool – a new BLAST tool with multi-BLAST capabilities. Input a single sequence or several FASTA-formatted sequences at once. Our tool uses NCBI BLAST to find genes, genomic sequences, ESTs, or PopSet Sequences that have BLAST similarity to your input sequence. BLASTbeta video introduction (4 min)
  • Usage and performance metrics for VEuPathDB sites are now updated monthly.
