--- layout: post title: SchistoDB 24 Released category: [SchistoDB] --- We are pleased to announce the release of SchistoDB in collaboration with the EuPathDB resources. This first release includes the genome sequence and annotation of Schistosoma haematobium Egypt and the assembled genome sequence of Schistosoma japonicum Anhui and Schistosoma mansoni Puerto Rico. Additional information about these genomes is available in the dataset section of SchistoDB. We look forward to working with you as we integrate additional genomes and functional data over the coming years.
