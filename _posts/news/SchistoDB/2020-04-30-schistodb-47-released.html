This is a draft news release that will be finalized upon Release 47 approval.

We are pleased to announce the release of SchistoDB 47.

The product of 6 months of back-end infrastructure work, Release 47 includes beta.schistodb.org in addition to our legacy sites. The beta site contains the same data and previous, as well as new, tools or functions in an updated and streamlined interface that emphasizes easy access to tools and help information (check out the new search functionality in the header).

Underlying our website's new look is a modernized client-server architecture. After three years of planning and development, the site is now driven completely by our new REST API. The browser-based user interface accesses that API to get all data, and is coded using the React/Redux framework.

Your feedback is invaluable as we improve our sites and transition to our new architecture and interface. So please try beta.schistodb.org and let us know how we are doing by completing one or all of the surveys below. Each takes about half an hour to complete.

  • Home page survey
  • Site search survey
  • Search strategy system survey

No new data in SchistoDB 46

New features in SchistoDB 46

  • Copy Number Variation Pipeline Bug Fix: To correct skewed copy number predictions, we changed the method for counting reads in our CNV pipeline from Cufflinks to HTSeq-Count. In our new pipeline we calculate FPKM manually. Please note - saved strategies that include CNV searches will likely produce different results in Release 46.
  • Our pipeline of genomic analyses includes open reading frame (ORFs) prediction in addition to loading gene models from the official annotation. Because data usage analysis showed low ORF usage, in our next release (47) predicted ORFs will be available as a genome-scale fasta file from our downloads section and removed from the searches and genome browser. Please Contact Us with questions or suggestions.
