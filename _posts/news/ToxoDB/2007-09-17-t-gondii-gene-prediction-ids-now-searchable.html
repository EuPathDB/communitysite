--- layout: post title: T. gondii gene prediction IDs now searchable category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,ToxoDB] --- Wherever in ToxoDB you can type an Annotated Gene's ID you can now also type the ID of an electronically predicted gene (eg, TgGlmHMM_1045). You will be taken to the associated annotated gene (eg, 27.m00868).
