--- layout: post title: ToxoDB 5.0 is released category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,ToxoDB] --- We are pleased to announce the release of ToxoDB 5.0. This is a major release of ToxoDB that includes several new large-scale datasets many of which have been graciously provided pre-publication to ToxoDB (Please see the data source section of ToxoDB for details and provider information). In addition, several new features and improvements have been implemented.

New datasets include:

  • Fully sequenced and annotated genomes of three strains of T. gondii - GT1 (Type I), ME49 (Type II) and VEG (Type III).
  • Genome sequence and annotation of the closely related Neospora caninum (annotation not fully complete yet).
  • Whole genome ChIP on chip analysis of three T. gondii strains.
  • Several proteomics datasets.
  • Microarray Expression profiling of the 3 archetypal T. gondii lineages.
New features include:
  • Redesigned result summary pages that accommodate the annotations from multiple strains of Toxoplasma and N. caninum. This should allow you to quickly access all or strain specific query results.
  • Synteny mapping is provided on gene pages and in the associated genome browser (gbrowse).
  • Improved annotations of Rhoptry proteins (ROPs) and SAG-related sequences (SRSs).
