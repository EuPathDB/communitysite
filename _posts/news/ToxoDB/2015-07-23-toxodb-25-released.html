We are pleased to announce the release of ToxoDB 25

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description as well as a list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

Genome Sequence

  • Cyclospora cayetanensis strain CHN_HEN01 — First release in ToxoDB. This genome sequence is provided pre-publication by Lihua Xiao, Centers for Disease Control, USA

Genome Annotation

  • Sarcocystis neurona SN3 — Annotation for this genome is now available and is provided pre-publication by Daniel K. Howe, University of Kentucky and Jessica Kissinger, University of Georgia.

Whole genome sequencing of isolate for SNP analysis

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - HhCatGer041 strain — Illumina genome sequencing of Hammondia hammondi isolate HhCatGer041 from Germany. Sequencing reads were aligned to Hammondia hammondi strain H.H.34 and used to determine SNPs. Walzer et al. - Jon Boyle, University of Pittsburg


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
