We are pleased to announce the release of ToxoDB 28

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

RNA sequencing

  • Gametocytes vs 2 Asexual Stages — Tanscriptomes of Eimeria tenella Houghton gametocytes compared to sporozoites and merozoites, determined by RNA sequencing Walker et al.

Proteomics

The following data sets were reloaded at the request of the data provider (J. Wastling) because the data analysis was significantly improved by applying multiple search engines. The new analysis produced more peptide/protein identifications and more reliable quantitation.
  • N. caninum Proteome During an Infection in Homo sapiens (Quant) — N. caninum peptide sequences from 6 proteomic analyses of parasite/host cell samples that were collected during time course infections in human foreskin fibroblasts. Wastling and Xia, unpublished
  • T. gondii Proteome During an Infection in H. sapiens (Quant) — T. gondii peptide sequences from 6 proteomic analyses of mixed parasite/host cell samples that were collected during time course infections in human foreskin fibroblasts. (6 time course experiments using 4 different strains of T. gondii) Wastling and Xia, unpublished
  • N. caninum Proteome During Infection in humans — N. caninum peptide sequences from a proteomic analysis of a mixed parasite/host sample collected during infection in human foreskin fibroblasts. Wastling and Xia, unpublished
  • T gondii Proteome During Infection in Homo sapiens — T. gondii peptide sequences from a proteomic analysis of a mixed parasite/host sample collected during infection in human foreskin fibroblasts. Multiple strains were analyzed (GT1 ME49 RH VEG) and all data were aligned to TgME49. Wastling and Xia, unpublished
