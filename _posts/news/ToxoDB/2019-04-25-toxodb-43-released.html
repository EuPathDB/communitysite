New data in ToxoDB 43

RNA Sequence

  • Transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent N. caninum strains — RNA sequence analysis of virulent (Nc-Spain7) and avirulent (Nc-Spain1H) N. caninum strains. This data was also aligned to the cow genome and the parasite data is available in HostDB. Horcajo et al. 2018
  • Transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent N caninum isolates during bovine infection — Transcriptomic analysis of two different isolates of Neospora caninum, the highly virulent isolate, Nc-Spain7, and the comparatively avirulent isolate, Nc-Spain1H. Mixed host-parasite samples of N. caninum infection 8 hours post infection of bovine trophoblast cell line F3 were analyzed. Bovine data is available in HostDB.
    Transcriptome of Bos taurus during infection with virulent and avirulent N. caninum strains. Horcajo et al. 2017
  • Transcriptome of wild-type and gra18 mutant strains of T. gondii — This data was also aligned to the mouse genome and the host data is available in HostDB.
    Transcriptome of mouse bone marrow derived macrophages infected by wild-type and gra18 mutant strains of T. gondii He et al.
  • Tachyzoite and merozoite transcriptomes — High-throughput RNA-Seq (SOLiD5) to compare the merozoite and tachyzoite transcriptomes from T. gondii CZ strain. Data are aligned to T. gondii ME49. Hehl et al.

Proteomics

  • Tachyzoite proteome (12, 36, 56 hpi) of virulent and avirulent N. caninum strains — Proteomic analysis of two Neospora caninum strains of disparate virulence: virulent Nc-Spain7 strain and the comparatively avirulent Nc-Spain1H strain. Samples taken at 12, 36 and 56 hours post infection were analyzed. Horcajo et al.
  • Quantiative tachyzoite proteome (12, 36, 56 hpi) of virulent and avirulent N. caninum strains — Quantitative proteomic analysis of two Neospora caninum strains of disparate virulence: virulent Nc-Spain7 strain and the comparatively avirulent Nc-Spain1H strain. Samples taken at 12, 36 and 56 hours post infection were analyzed. Horcajo et al.

ChIP Sequence

  • Versatile acetylation-methylation switch at K31 of histone H4 — Versatile modifications at Lys31 within the globular domain of histone H4 that crucially determine genome organization and expression in Apicomplexa parasites. Sindikubwabo et al.

New features in ToxoDB 43

  • A beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser is now available from gene record pages on any EuPathDB site.
