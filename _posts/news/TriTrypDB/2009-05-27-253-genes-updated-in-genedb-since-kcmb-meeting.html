--- layout: post title: 253 genes updated in GeneDB since KCMB meeting category: [TriTrypDB] --- We received many user comments during the KCMB meeting and following. Many of these have already been examined by TriTrypDB curators (at GeneDB, SBRI and UGA) and incorporated into the official annotation. 253 genes have been modified since the meeting resulting in a total of 710 genes that have been updated since this current build of TriTrypDB.
