We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 5.0

New Data:

Detailed information, descriptions, citations and links to specific searches are available in the Dataset section.

  • Updated genome sequences and annotation:
    • Trypanosoma brucei 927 has been updated to version 5.
    • Trypanosoma cruzi Sylvio X10 assembly has been updated.
    • Leishmania braziliensis MHOM/BR/75/M2903 assembly updated.
    • Leishmania major strain SD 75.1 assembly updated.
    • Trypanosoma cruzi JR cl. 4 assembly updated.

  • New genomes:
    • Leishmania donovanii BPK282A1 sequence and annotation.
    • Trypanosoma cruzi marinkellei sequence and annotation.

  • Single nucleotide polymorphyism data from High throughput sequencing:
    • L. braziliensis MHOM/BR/75/M2904 Aligned SNP calls for strain M2903
    • L. braziliensis MHOM/BR/75/M2904 Aligned SNP calls for strain M2904
    • L. donovani BPK282A1 Aligned SNP calls for 16 clinical isolates
    • L. infantum JPCM5 Aligned SNP calls for strain LV9
    • L. major strain Friedlin Aligned SNP calls for strain LV39
    • L. major strain Friedlin Aligned SNP calls for strains LV39cl5 and Seidman751
    • L. mexicana MHOM/GT/2001/U1103 Aligned SNP calls for strain U1103
    • T. brucei TREU927 Aligned HTS SNP calls for Lister strain 427
    • T. brucei TREU927 Aligned SNP calls for strain STIB247
    • T. brucei TREU927 Aligned SNP calls for strain TREU927
    • T. brucei TREU927 Aligned SNP calls for strain gambiense
    • T. brucei gambiense DAL972 Aligned SNP calls for strain DAL972
    • T. cruzi CL Brener Esmeraldo-like Aligned SNP calls for strains Cl3 and JRc14
    • T. cruzi CL Brener Non-Esmeraldo-like Aligned SNP calls for strains Cl3 and JRc14
    • T. cruzi strain CL Brener Aligned SNP calls for strains Cl3 and JRc14

  • Proteomics data:
    • T. brucei TREU927 Bloodstream form Flagellum Surface and Matrix Proteomes
    • T. brucei TREU927 Total Flagellar Proteome from bloodstream form
    • T. brucei TREU927 [Bloodstream form enriched kinome]
    • T. brucei TREU927 Comparative proteomics of bloodstream and procyclic forms (T.b. 427)
    • T. cruzi (Dm28c) Phosphoproteome during Metocyclogenesis
    • SUMOylated proteome of T. cruzi CL Brener epimastigotes

  • RNA sequence data: 
    • Promastigote transcriptome of L. infantum JPCM5 by mRNA-Seq

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary.  Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ã˜) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
