The latest release of TriTrypDB includes new versions of many of the underlying genomes.  To view a summary of the changes in genes and genomes please examine these comparison tables.

Please note that following release of the latest genome versions some issues were discovered primarily with chromosomes T. brucei 9 and 11.  These include incorrect gene models for an estimated 50 genes and inappropriately dropped annotation (gene names).  These issues are being addressed by the annotators at GeneDB and will be made available in TriTrypDB once ready.  In the meantime, the previous version of TriTrypDB is available for reference.

Current work is focusing on continued updates to gene annotation based on user comments and literature.  In addition, contigs not assigned to chromosomes (known as bin chromosomes) will be made available for T. brucei (this includes a chromosome 11 bin -- contigs that can be assigned to this chromosome but order and orientation is unknown) and T. brucei gambiense.  Telomeric sequences obtained through TAR cloning targeting the T. brucei  927 and 427 telomeres are also now available for download and will be included in a future release of TriTrypDB.  

Please let  us know if you have any questions or comments.
