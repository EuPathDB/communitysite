We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 9.0



New data in this release:

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

 

New Annotated Genomes:

Trypanosoma cruzi Dm28c – first release in TriTrypDB (Grisard et al.; Genbank version Nov 13, 2013).

Trypanosoma rangeli SC58 – first release in TriTrypDB (Stoco et al.; Genbank version Oct 30, 2013).

 

Updated Genomes:

Trypanosoma cruzi CL Brener -- re-annotated (GeneDB version Sept 16, 2014).

Trypanosoma cruzi CL Brener Esmeraldo-like -- re-annotated (GeneDB version Sept 16, 2014).

Trypanosoma cruzi CL Brener Non-Esmeraldo-like -- re-annotated (GeneDB version Sept 16, 2014).

Trypanosoma evansi STIB 805 -- annotation corrected (Schnaufer Lab; version Jun 3, 2014).

 

Functional Annotation Updated:

Annotation updated: products, GO terms, EC numbers, gene names and synonyms.

L. braziliensis MHOM/BR/75/M2904 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 325 genes with updated annotations.

L. donovani BPK282A1 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 295 genes with updated annotations.

L. infantum JPCM5 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 247 genes with updated annotations.

L. major strain Friedlin -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 326 genes with updated annotations.

L. mexicana MHOM/GT/2001/U1103 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 233 genes with updated annotations.

Trypanosoma brucei 927 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014): 1078 genes with updated annotations.

T. brucei gambiense DAL972 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 262 genes with updated annotations.

Trypanosoma congolense IL3000 -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014): 257 genes with updated annotations.

Trypanosoma cruzi CL Brener -- updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 1549 genes with updated annotations.

Trypanosoma vivax Y486 - updated (GeneDB version Nov 20, 2014) 233 genes with updated annotations.

 

RNA Sequencing:

Procyclic and bloodstream form transcriptomes and ribosome profiling (Jensen et al.) – Ribosome profiling and mRNA sequencing in animal-derived slender bloodstream forms (T. brucei 927) and insect midgut cultured procyclic forms (T. brucei Lister 427).

 

Improvements and updates:

BLAST was updated to the latest NCBI version 2.2.30.

GBrowse performance is improved following EuPathDB infrastructure changes.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
