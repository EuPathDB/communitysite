We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 25

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Leptomonas pyrrhocoris H10 — LukesLab version 5 May 2014. This genome sequence and annotation are available for the first time in TriTrypDB and are provided pre-publication by Julius Lukes, Czech Academy of Sciences.
  • Leptomonas seymouri ATCC 30220 — YurchenkoLab version 6 May 2014. This genome sequence and annotation are available for the first time in TriTrypDB and are provided pre-publication by Vyacheslav Yurchenko, University of Ostrava.

Genome Annotation

Annotation for the following genomes is now available in TriTrypDB. These genomes from the Trypanosomatid sequencing white paper project were annotated in collaboration with The Sanger Institute's Parasite Genomics Group.
  • Crithidia fasciculata Cf-Cl
  • Leishmania major LV39c5
  • Leishmania major SD 75.1
  • Endotrypanum monterogeii LV88

RNA Sequencing

  • Transcriptome of total- and polyA RNA fraction (LpyrH10) — Illumina RNA sequencing of total- and polyA-RNA fractions from Leptomonas pyrrhocoris H10. Provided pre-publication by Julius Lukes, Czech Academy of Sciences.
  • Transcriptome at 23C and 35C growth conditions — Transcriptome of L. seymouri ATCC 30220 at 23 and 35C growth conditions determined by Illumina sequencing. Provided pre-publication by Vyacheslav Yurchenko, University of Ostrava.

Proteomics

  • Contractile Vacuole Proteome, epimatigote (CL Brener) — Proteomic analysis of a T. cruzi CL Brener fraction enriched for contractile vacuoles and analyzed by one-dimensional gel electrophoresis and LC-MS/MS T. cruzi CL Brener. Provided by Roberto Docampo, University of Georgia; Ulrich et al..
  • Acidocalcisome proteome, procyclic form (Tb927) — Proteome of acidocalcisome fractions from Trypanosoma brucei TREU927 procyclic form parasites. Provided by Roberto Docampo, University of Georgia; Huang et al..

Whole genome sequencing of isolates for SNP analysis

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - L. major isolate V1 — Illumina genome sequencing of Leishmania major Friedlin isolate V1. Sequencing reads were aligned to Leishmania major Friedlin and used to determine SNPs. Stephen M. Beverley, Washington University in St. Louis.
  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - 3 L. donovani Indian Isolates — Illumina genome sequencing of Leishmania donovani BHU-1095, 39, and 2001. Sequencing reads were aligned to Leishmania donovani BPK282A1 and used to determine SNPs. Singh et al.
  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - 12 L. infantum hybrids — Illumina genome sequencing of 11 Leishmania infantum isolates from sand flies and 1 patient isolate in a focus of cutaneous leishmaniasis in the Cukurova province of southeast Turkey. Sequencing reads were aligned to Leishmania infantum JPCM5 and used to determine SNPs. Rogers et al.
  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - L. donovani Sri Lanka Isolates — Illumina genome sequencing of Sri Lankan isolates MON-37CL, and MON-37VL. Sequencing reads were aligned to Leishmania donovani BPK282A1 and used to determine SNPs. Ranasinghe et al.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
