We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 26

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

Proteomics

  • Intact flagellar proteome of procyclic form (Tb427) — Proteome of detached intact flagella from Trypanosoma brucei Lister 427 procyclic form parasites. Peptides are aligned to T. brucei TREU 927. Subota et al.

Whole genome sequencing of isolates for SNP analysis

  • Aligned genomic sequence reads - strain Cf-Cl — Crithidia fasciculata strain Cf-Cl high throughput DNA sequencing data was aligned to the Crithidia fasciculata Cf-Cl reference and used to determine SNPs. (Stephen M. Beverley, Washington University in St. Louis)

Comparative Genomics

  • Promastigote Form Origins of Replication — Origins of replication in L. major Friedlin and L. mexicana MHOMGT2001U1103 promastigotes determined by Marker Frequency Analysis, a technique that compares frequency of marker sequences in replicating and non-replicating cells. Whole genome DNAseq of FACS sorted promastigotes is presented as the ratio (early S and late S vs G2-phase) of the frequencies of markers (DNAseq reads) in 2.5 kb bins. (Richard McCulloch - The Wellcome Trust Centre for Molecular Parasitology)
  • Procyclic Form Origins of Replication — Origins of replication in T.brucei TREU927 Procyclic form determined by Marker Frequency Analysis, a technique that compares frequency of marker sequences in replicating and non-replicating cells. Whole genome DNAseq of FACS sorted procyclic cells is presented as the ratio (early S and late S, or early-mid S phase vs G2-phase) of the frequencies of markers (DNAseq reads) in 2.5 kb bins. (Richard McCulloch - The Wellcome Trust Centre for Molecular Parasitology)
