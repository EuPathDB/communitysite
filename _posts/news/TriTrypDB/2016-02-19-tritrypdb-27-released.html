We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 27

New data in this release

New RNA Sequencing data

  • mRNA Decay in Bloodstream and Procyclic Forms — The decay of T. brucei TREU 927 mRNA in bloodstream and procyclic forms was determined by sequencing RNA that was harvested at various times from cultures incubated in ActinomycinD to inhibit transcription. Fadda et al.

New literature references on gene pages

  • TriTrypDB gene pages now include the PMID references available from GeneDB's ongoing curation efforts. See the PubMed table in the Annotation section of gene pages for literature references that are current with the GeneDB October 5, gff versions.

New Features

New Tutorials

  • Exploring expression distributions with our Percentile Ranking searches - Percentile ranking is one way that EuPathDB analyzes expression data. This tutorial describes our percentile ranking of expression data and the searches that we offer based on that analysis.
  • Finding Differentially expressed genes based on Fold Change with a P-value - One way EuPathDB analyzes expression is to determine statistically significant differential gene expression using the Fisher's exact test. This tutorial describes our Fold Change w/ P-value searches that return differentially expressed genes based on the level of statistical confidence that you choose.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
