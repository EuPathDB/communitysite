We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 30

New data in TriTrypDB 30

Quantitative Proteomics

  • Purine Stress Response in Promastigotes – Quantitative proteomic comparison (AMT tag method) of purine-rich and purine-starved L. donovani promastigotes at 6, 12, 24, and 48h. Data are analyzed against the L. infantum JPCM5 genome. Martin et al.

Cellular Localization Imaging

  • Cellular Localization of tagged T. brucei proteins – Images and localization information associated with GO terms obtained from the TrypTag project. Access the data on gene pages (example) and through a dedicated search based on GO terms (Identify Genes based on Cellular Localization Imaging).
