We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 32

New data in TriTrypDB 32

Annotated Genome

  • Trypanosoma cruzi Sylvio X10/1 — Trypanosoma cruzi Sylvio X10 was sequenced to 30X coverage using 454 and Illumina HiSeq technologies. Appears for the first time in TriTrypDB. Frazen et a..

Updated Functional Annotation

Functional annotations in TriTrypDB 32 are current with GeneDB March 2017 GFF versions. Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, EC numbers, gene names, synonyms and PMIDs.

RNA Sequencing

  • Promastigote, Axenic Amastigote and Intracellular Amastigote Transcriptomes — Illumina sequencing of poly-A selected RNA of L. mexicana MHOMGT2001U1103 promastigotes, axenic amastigotes and intracellular amastigotes. Fiebig et al..

Proteomics

  • Glycosomal proteome — Glycosome proteome for procyclic form Trypanosoma brucei by epitope-tag organelle enrichment. Guther et al..

Cellular Localization Imaging

  • Cellular Localization of tagged T. brucei proteins – Additional images and localization information associated with GO terms obtained from the TrypTag project. Access the data on gene pages (example) and through a dedicated search based on GO terms (Identify Genes based on Cellular Localization Imaging).


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
