We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 38

New data in TriTrypDB 38

Proteomics and post-translational modifications

    Acetylation proteomes of T. cruzi and T. brucei lysine and N-terminal amino acids. Moretti et al.
  • T. brucei TREU927 bloodstream and procyclic form data
  • T. cruzi CL Brener Non-Esmeraldo-like epimastigote data
  • T. cruzi CL Brener Esmeraldo-like epimastigote data
  • T. cruzi strain CL Brener epimastigote data

ChIP Sequencing

  • RNA pol II, H2A.Z and H3 genome-wide distribution — Genome-wide distribution of RNA pol II, H2A.Z and H3 based on ChIP-seq against endogenously tagged RPB9 (RNA pol II subunit), MNase-ChIP-seq using a custom-made antibodies against H2A.Z and H3. Wedel et al.
