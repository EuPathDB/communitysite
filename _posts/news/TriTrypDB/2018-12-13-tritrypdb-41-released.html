We are pleased to announce the release of TriTrypDB 41

New data in TriTrypDB 41

RNA Sequencing

  • Comparative transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi (CLBrener aligned) —
    Transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi determined by RNA sequencing. Belew et al. This data set has corresponding host data at HostDB: HFF transcriptional response to virulent and avirulent T. cruzi.
  • Comparative transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi (Esmo aligned) —
    Transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi determined by RNA sequencing. Belew et al. This data set has corresponding host data at HostDB: HFF transcriptional response to virulent and avirulent T. cruzi.
  • Comparative transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi (Non-Esmo aligned) —
    Transcriptomes of virulent and avirulent T. cruzi determined by RNA sequencing. Belew et al. This data set has corresponding host data at HostDB: HFF transcriptional response to virulent and avirulent T. cruzi.
  • Transcriptome of T. brucei rhodesiense from human, rodent, culture — Transcriptome of T. brucei rhodesiense from humans and rodents determined by RNA sequencing. Mulindwa et al.

Proteomics

  • Quantitative proteomics of T. brucei differentiation — Quant proteomics of Trypanosoma brucei AnTat1.1 differentiation with time points at 0, 2, 4, 6, 12, 24, and 48 hours. Dejung et al.

Transcription Start Sites - GBrowse Track

  • Sequencing of small 5'-triphosphate-containing RNA (transcription start sites) —
    Transcription start site data is integrated as a GBRowse track. The data represent a COVERnant analysis of RNA sequence data. Wedel et al. View data in GBRowse

New features in TriTrypDB 41

Introducing Clustal Omega


EuPathDB sites are now using Clustal Omega for gene page protein alignments in the 'Orthologs and Paralogs within EuPathDB' table and the 'Retrieve multiple sequence alignment or multi-FASTA' tool in the 'Orthology and synteny' section of gene pages. The 'Isolate Alignments in this Gene Region' tool still uses CulstalW.

New Gene page tools

  • Interpro —
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence for sequence analysis and classification via InterPro.
  • GPI anchor prediction —
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence for GPI modification site prediction via the GPI Lipid Anchor Project.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
