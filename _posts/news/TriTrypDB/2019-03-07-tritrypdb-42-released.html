New data in TriTrypDB 42

Genome Sequence and Annotation - These annotated genomes appear for the first time in TriTrypDB.

  • Bodo saltans strain Lake Konstanz – This Bodo saltans genome sequence and annotation represents a draft version. Please carefully consider gene models and genome structure before drawing conclusions. NCBI Bioproject.
  • Leishmania donovani CL-SL – PacBio genome sequence and annotation of a clinical isolate of L. donovani causing cutaneous leishmaniasis from Sri Lanka. Lypaczewski et al. Zhangi et al.
  • Trypanosoma cruzi TCC – Genome sequence and annotation for Trypanosoma cruzi TCC sequenced with PacBio Single Molecule Real-Time technology and Illumina methods. Berná et al.

Additional Version of Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Trypanosoma brucei Lister 427 (2018) – Genome sequence and annotation for Trypanosoma brucei Lister 427 (2018) sequenced with SMRT sequencing technology (PacBio) to ~100X coverage. Müller et al.
  • Trypanosoma cruzi Dm28c 2018 – Genome sequence and annotation for Trypanosoma cruzi Dm28c sequenced with PacBio Single Molecule Real-Time technology. Berná et al.

Renamed Versions of Genome Sequence and Annotation - To distinguish the three versions of T. cruzi Dm28c in TriTrypDB, these versions are renamed.

  • Trypanosoma cruzi Dm28c 2014 – This genome sequence and annotation was previously called Trypanosoma cruzi Dm28c. It was sequenced using 454 technology. Grisard et al. NCBI Bioproject
  • Trypanosoma cruzi Dm28c 2017 – This genome sequence and annotation was previously called Trypanosoma cruzi cruzi strain Dm28c. It was sequenced with Pacific Biosciences SMRT technology using P5-C3 chemistry. NCBI Bioproject.

Newly Annotated Genomes

  • Leishmania amazonensis MHOM/BR/71973/M2269 – This genome was annotated at EuPathDB using the Companion genome annotation pipeline. The genome was downloaded from Genbank and was sequences with a combination of 454 and Illumina GAII technologies. Real et al. NCBI Bioproject

RNA sequence data

  • Adipose tissue vs bloodstream form transcriptomes – Transcriptomes of T brucei parasites isolated from mouse blood on day 4 pi and gonadal fat on day 6 pi. Trindade et al.
  • Leishmania amazonensis RNAseq during human macrophage infection – RNAseq analysis of transcriptomes of human macrophage and Leishmania amazonensis during an infection time course. This study was performed on mixed parasite/host samples and has associated data sets for L. major in TriTrypDB and human in HostDB (Leishmania major and Leishmania amazonensis RNAseq during human macrophage infection). Fernandes et al.
  • Transcriptomes of T. brucei culture-derived slender/stumpy bloodstream and early/late procyclic forms – RNA-Seq analysis was performed on defined cultures of slender bloodstream forms, stumpy bloodstream forms and early and late procyclic forms. Naguleswaran et al.

Whole Genome Sequencing of Isolates

  • Whole genome sequencing of T. cruzi TcI isolates and clones – Provided prepublication from Bjorn Andersson lab, Department of Cell and Molecular Biology, Karolinska Institute. NCBI Bioproject

ChIP Seq data

  • Leishmania major KKT1 Chip-Seq (Centromeres) – Chromatin immunoprecipitation coupled to high-throughput sequencing (ChIP-Seq) of L. major KKT1 (LmKKT1) clearly identified one major peak per chromosome. Garcia-Silva et al.

New features in TriTrypDB 42

  • Updated Alignment Tool on Gene Pages –
    The Clustal Omega alignment tool in the 'Orthologs and Paralogs within EuPathDB' table now accommodates protein, CDS and genomic sequence alignments. To run Clustal Omega, select genes from the table. Then choose the sequence type and initiate the alignment with the 'Run Clutal Omega for selected genes' button. Output formats include Mismatches highlighted, FASTA, PHYLIP, STOCKHOLM, and VIENNA. This tool replaces the 'Retrieve multiple sequence alignment or multi-FASTA' tool.
  • Beta access to JBrowse –
    A beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser, is available from gene pages. Use the JBrowse Genome Browser link under the graphic Shortcuts on the gene page. Example Link Location
  • New Gene Page STRING tool –
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence to the STRING functional protein association network. Szklarczyk et al.
  • Result Table Improvements –
    Result column configurations and sorting are now saved for each strategy step and will be retained when you come back or when you share a strategy with someone else.
  • New Options for FASTA downloads –
    The header line of FASTA formatted downloads can be configured to contain only the gene ID. The sequence can be configured to appear on a signle line or the standard 60 characters per line.
Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
