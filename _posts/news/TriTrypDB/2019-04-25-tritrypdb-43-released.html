New data in TriTrypDB 43

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • Leishmania donovani strain LV9 – PacBio genome sequence and annotation. Annotation was performed using Companion using L. donovani BPK282A1 as reference. These data are provided unpublished from the Berriman Group at the Wellcome Sanger Institute.

Unannotated Genome Sequence

  • Trypanosoma cruzi strain 231 – Baptista et al. and Reis-Cunha et al.
  • Trypanosoma cruzi Bug2148 – Callejas-Hernández et al. Jan 2018 and Callejas-Hernández et al. Oct 2018.
  • Trypanosoma cruzi Y – Callejas-Hernández et al. Oct 2018.

Whole Genome Sequencing of Isolates

  • Genomic diversity in Leishmania donovani in Indian subcontinent – Whole genome sequences from clinical isolates were used to track the evolution and epidemiology of L. donovani from the Indian subcontinent. Imamura et al.

Updated Functional Annotation

Summary of annotation updates which include products, GO terms, EC numbers, gene names, synonyms, publications, external database references and notes from curators. These functional annotations are current with GeneDB April 2019 GFF versions.

New features in TriTrypDB 43

  • A beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser is now available from gene record pages on any EuPathDB site.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct errors in old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
