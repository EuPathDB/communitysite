New features in TriTrypDB 45

  • All EuPathDB sites now offer the JBrowse Genome Browser. GBrowse will be phased out in coming releases. Please Contact Us with comments and suggestions for the genome browser.
  • The new RNASeq Transcript Summary tool on gene pages helps reveal experiments in which the gene is highly regulated. The tool displays the gene's FPKM values for all experiments in TriTrypDB and offers features such as log/linear scaling, zoom and pan to explore the gene's expression in each experiment.
Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary.
