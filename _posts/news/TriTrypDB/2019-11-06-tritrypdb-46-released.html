New data in TriTrypDB 46

Newly integrated genome sequence and annotation

  • Leishmania braziliensis strain MHOM/BR/75/M2904 2019 — This PacBio genome with maxicircle contigs appears for the first time in TriTrypDB. These data were produced by the Parasite Genomics Group at Wellcome Sanger Institute. The previous version of this genome Leishmania braziliensis strain MHOM/BR/75/M2904 is still available and fully supported.
  • Trypanosoma congolense strain IL3000 2019 — This PacBio genome appears for the first time in TriTrypDB. Abbas et al. The previous version of this genome Trypanosoma congolense IL3000 is still available and fully supported.
  • Trypanosoma cruzi Brazil A4 — This PacBio and Illumina sequenced genome appears for the first time in TriTrypDB and is offered pre-publication from the Rick Tarleton Lab.
  • Trypanosoma cruzi YC6 — This PacBio and Illumina sequenced genome appears for the first time in TriTrypDB and is offered pre-publication from the Rick Tarleton Lab.

New features in TriTrypDB 46

  • Copy Number Variation Pipeline Bug Fix: To correct skewed copy number predictions, we changed the method for counting reads in our CNV pipeline from Cufflinks to HTSeq-Count. In our new pipeline we calculate FPKM manually. Please note - saved strategies that include CNV searches will likely produce different results in Release 46.
  • Our pipeline of genomic analyses includes open reading frame (ORF) prediction in addition to loading gene models from the official annotation. Because data usage analysis showed low ORF usage, in our next release (47) predicted ORFs will be available as a genome-scale fasta file from our downloads section and removed from the searches and genome browser. Please Contact Us with questions or suggestions.
