--- layout: post title: GiardiaDB and TrichDB published in Nucleic Acids Research category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,TrichDB] --- We are pleased to announce the publication of the database paper introducing both GiardiaDB and TrichDB in the database issue of Nucleic Acid Research.

GiardiaDB and TrichDB: integrated genomic resources for the eukaryotic protist pathogens Giardia lamblia and Trichomonas vaginalis. Nucleic Acids Research Advance Access published on September 29, 2008, DOI 10.1093/nar/gkn631.
