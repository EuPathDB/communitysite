We are pleased to announce the release of TrichDB 45

No new data in TrichDB 45

New features in TrichDB 45

  • All EuPathDB sites now offer the JBrowse Genome Browser. GBrowse will be phased out in coming releases. Please Contact Us with comments and suggestions for the genome browser.
  • The new RNASeq Transcript Summary tool on gene pages helps reveal experiments in which the gene is highly regulated. The tool displays the gene's FPKM values for all experiments in TrichDB and offers features such as log/linear scaling, zoom and pan to explore the gene's expression in each experiment.


Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
