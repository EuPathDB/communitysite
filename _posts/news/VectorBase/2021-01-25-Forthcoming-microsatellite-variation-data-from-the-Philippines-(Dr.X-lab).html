--- layout: post title: Forthcoming microsatellite variation data from the Philippines (Dr. Koz Watanabe lab) category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,VectorBase] ---

Microsatellite data from Metropolitan Manila, Philippines, corresponding to a forthcoming publication from the Molecular Ecology and Health (MEcoH) Lab will be made publicly available in a forthcoming VectorBase release. This project presents the influence of roads on the fine-scale population genetic structure of the dengue vector, Aedes aegypti (Linnaeus).

It has been pre-assigned Population Biology Project ID VBP0000716.

If you have any further enquiries please contact the VectorBase helpdesk at help@vectorbase.org.
