--- layout: post title: Forthcoming microsatellite variation data from Sudan (Dr. Salim lab) category: [EuPathDB,VEuPathDB,VectorBase] ---

Microsatellite data from Sudan corresponding to a forthcoming publication from Bashir Salim (Project PI) from the University of Khartoum, Sudan. This data set will be made publicly available in a forthcoming VectorBase release.

The research authors used 12 microsatellites loci to examine the population genetic and population history of Aedes aegypti in Sudan. The mosquito samples were collected from eastern Sudan (Kassala and Port Sudan cities) and western Sudan (Al Fashir and Nyala cities), during November 2018- February 2020.

It has been pre-assigned MapVEu (Population Biology) project ID VBP0000762.
