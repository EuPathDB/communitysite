General Information

TriTrypDB is an integrated genomic and functional genomic database for pathogens of the family Trypanosomatidae, including organisms in both Leishmania and Trypanosoma genera. TriTrypDB and its continued development are possible through the collaborative efforts between EuPathDB, GeneDB and colleagues at the Seattle Biomedical Research Institute (SBRI).

For a list of the data integrated into TriTrypDB and the related publications please see Data Sets.

Please check our annual EuPathDB Workshop at UGA exercises to see the scope of data and functionality in our sites.
Citing Data Providers

Much of the data in TriTrypDB is provided by independent researchers. For a list of the data integrated into TriTrypDB and the related publications see Data Sets. Please cite the authors/providers when you use their data.

Funding

Funding for TriTrypDB development and enhanced annotation of kinetoplastid parasites is provided by coordinated grants from the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and the Wellcome Trust.
TriTrypDB joins the family of pathogen-databases that are housed under the NIAID-funded EuPathDB Bioinformatics Resource Center (BRC) umbrella. The EuPathDB team is mainly distributed between the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and the University of Georgia in Athens, Georgia.
Data Access Policy

The data at this site is provided freely for public use, through the contributions of many researchers including those involved in generating the genome sequences. The data is not guaranteed to be accurate as it often reflects the results of ongoing research. When using data obtained from any EuPathDB project, it is important to cite the original publications and contributors of that data. Please see Citing TriTrypDB below.
