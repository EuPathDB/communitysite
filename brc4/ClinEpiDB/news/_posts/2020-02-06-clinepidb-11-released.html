We are pleased to announce the release of ClinEpiDB 11!

New Features in this Release

  • We have compiled a list of answers to frequently asked questions, such as how to cite datasets accessed via ClinEpiDB in your publications and how to submit your own dataset for upload onto our website. To access our 'Frequently Asked Questions' page, hover over "About" on the header and click FAQ.
  • We have added links to external 'Global Health Data Resources' on our 'Tutorials and Resources' page, such as sites providing education on data science and epidemiology, data management best practices, and visualizations of global health data. To access these links, hover over "About" on the homepage header and click Tutorials and Resources.
  • Improvements have been made to the histograms for variables with continuous data. You can now interact with how the data in these histograms is displayed by selecting the range of the data you would like included on the x-axis, change the size of the interval bins, zoom in or out by adjusting the y-axis, or choose to display on a log or linear scale.

New Data in this Release

  • The SCORE Mozambique S. haematobium Cluster Randomized Trial was coordinated by the Schistosomiasis Consortium for Operational Research and Evaluation (SCORE). The study enrolled over 81,000 participants from 150 villages in Mozambique to evaluate alternative approaches to mass drug administration (MDA) in communities with high prevalence rates of schistosomiasis.
