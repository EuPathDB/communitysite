We are pleased to announce the release of MicrobiomeDB 14

New data in this release

  • There is no new data in this release.

New features in this release

  • All studies are now searchable via a text box on the landing page, making it easy to quickly identify studies of interest.
  • Shiny apps are now interactive! Static ggplot2 graphics have been replaced with plotly graphics, allowing users to select, zoom and interact with plots in new and exciting ways.
Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
