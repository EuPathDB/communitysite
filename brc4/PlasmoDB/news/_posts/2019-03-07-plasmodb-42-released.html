New data in PlasmoDB 42

Genome Sequence and Annotation

  • P. vivax-like Pvl01 – This genome appears for the first time in PlasmoDB and was downloaded from the Wellcome Sanger Institute. Gilabert et al.

Proteomics

  • Apicoplast and ER Proteomes (Quantitative) – Apicoplast proteome determined by BioID followed by mass spec of P. falciparum Dd2 parasites during asexual blood-stage growth. Boucher et al.

ChIP Sequence

  • Genome-wide HP1 occupancy in P. berghei schizonts – Fraschka et al.
  • Genome-wide HP1 occupancy in P. chabaudi schizonts – Fraschka et al.
  • Genome-wide HP1 Occupancy in P. falciparum strains – Genome-wide distribution of HP1 in P. falciparum 3D7, NF54, NF135, Pf2004 schizonts, as well as Pf3D7 ring, Pf3D7 trophozoites, Pf2004 gameotcyte II/III and Pf2004 gametocyte IV/V. Fraschka et al.
  • Genome-wide HP1 occupancy in P. knowlesi – Genome-wide occupancy of HP1 in schizonts from P. knowlesi A1-C.1 and A1-H.1. Fraschka et al.
  • Genome-wide HP1 occupancy in P. vivax clinical isolate pool – Fraschka et al.
  • Genome-wide HP1 occupancy in P. yoelii yoelii YM schizonts – Fraschka et al.

Whole Genome Sequencing of Isolates

  • Whole genome sequencing of P. vivax-like isolates – A total of 11 P. vivax-like samples were obtained from infected chimpanzee blood samples and an infected mosquito collected in Gabon. Gilabert et al.
  • African Ape Plasmodium vivax isolates – Whole genome sequence data of P. vivax strains infecting six chimpanzees Loyt et al.

Protein Microarray

  • Antibody array of recent exposure to Plasmodium infection – This study assessed responses to 856 P. falciparum antigens by protein microarray in 186 Ugandan children, for whom detailed P. falciparum exposure data were available. Helb et al.

New features in PlasmoDB 42

  • Updated Alignment Tool on Gene Pages –
    The Clustal Omega alignment tool in the 'Orthologs and Paralogs within EuPathDB' table now accommodates protein, CDS and genomic sequence alignments. To run Clustal Omega, select genes from the table. Then choose the sequence type and initiate the alignment with the 'Run Clutal Omega for selected genes' button. Output formats include Mismatches highlighted, FASTA, PHYLIP, STOCKHOLM, and VIENNA. This tool replaces the 'Retrieve multiple sequence alignment or multi-FASTA' tool.
  • Beta access to JBrowse –
    A beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser, is available from gene pages. Use the JBrowse Genome Browser link under the graphic Shortcuts on the gene page. Example Link Location
  • New Gene Page STRING tool –
    The 'Protein features and properties' section now includes an option to submit the protein sequence to the STRING functional protein association network. Szklarczyk et al.
  • Result Table Improvements –
    Result column configurations and sorting are now saved for each strategy step and will be retained when you come back or when you share a strategy with someone else.
  • New Options for FASTA downloads –
    The header line of FASTA formatted downloads can be configured to contain only the gene ID. The sequence can be configured to appear on a signle line or the standard 60 characters per line.
