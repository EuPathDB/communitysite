We are pleased to announce the release of ToxoDB 11.0

New data in this release:
  • Toxoplasma gondii ME49 transcriptomes of cat enteroepithelial stages: This data was funded by the EuPathDB driving biological project mechanism and constitutes the first whole genome transcriptomic data from cat stages.  This data has been provided prepublication by Dr. Adrian Hehl and collaborators.  View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section.  
  • Toxoplasma gondii VEG updated genome sequence and annotation: This data was generated as part of the Toxoplasma sequencing white paper project.   View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section. 

  • RNA sequence data from T. gondii GT1 and ME49 strains: This data was generated as part of the Toxoplasma sequencing white paper project and was used in improving and updating gene models. View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section. 

  • Updated Toxoplasma isolate data based on RFLP typing: This isolate data is an updates the previously available data from Dr. Chunlei Su. View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section. 

  • Toxoplasma isolate data based on RFLP typing: This data has been provided prepublication (Casartelli-Alves et al., unpublished).
    View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section. 

  • Updated transcriptome data from Toxoplasma strains during murine macrophage infection (Minot et al.):  Additional strains were integrated into this dataset. View experimental details and available searches in the dataset section. 

  • Eimeria genomes and annotation: A number of Eimeria genomes and annotations are now available in ToxoDB.  These genomes were provided prepublication by Drs. Arnab Pain and Adam Reid.  The genomes include:
    • E. acervulina Houghton
    • E. brunetti Houghton
    • E. maxima Weybridge
    • E. mitis Houghton
    • E. necatrix Houghton
    • E. praecox Houghton
    • E. tenella  Houghton. 

    • View experimental details and links to searches in the dataset section. 

  • Eimeria falciformis Bayer Haberkorn 1970 genome and annotation: This genome sequence and annotation has been provided prepublication by Dr. Christoph Dieterich.  View experimental details and links to searches in the dataset section. 


New features:
  • Search genes based on polymorphism data: The high throughput sequencing SNP searches have been upgraded to increase their speed and provide additional search functionality. Meta data associated with samples is now  more intuitively organized. Search for genes based on SNP characteristics or identify SNPs based on multiple criteria including within one group of isolates or between two groups of isolates.
  • Filter samples based on metadata: It is easier now to tailor your searches (such as SNP searches) by defining samples based on their metadata characteristics.

 New Tutorials:

Learn how to upload custom tracks in the genome browser.

  • Transform by Orthology Across Taxon Groups 
  • Transform by Orthology within a Taxon Group
  • Orthology information on gene pages
  • Inferring UTR length using RNA sequencing data
  • Decreasing Gene Page Load Time

Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary.  Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits. When search parameters change with a new release, we invalidate (Ø) the search and ask you to rerun it. When IDs are updated or removed, we map the old IDs to the new ones, remove the old IDs from your Basket, and leave your Favorites page alone.
