We are pleased to announce the release of GiardiaDB 26

New data in this release

The data set title below links to a full description and list of associated searches and GBrowse tracks.

Subcellular Localization Images

  • Cellular Localization of Trophozoite or Cytoskeletal GFP-fused proteins — Subcellular localization of selected trophozoite or cytoskeletal proteins from Giardia Assemblage A isolate WB determined by live imaging of GFP-fusion strains. Images for 108 new genes added, making a total of 269 genes analyzed. ( Hagen et al.)

New Features

GBrowse Track Search

  • The Select Tracks page in the Genome Browser now includes a search function. Search for specific tracks based on a word or phrase from the track title or category. image
