New data in GiardiaDB 44

Unannotated Genomes

  • Giardia Assemblage A isolate AS175 – Franzen et al.
  • Giardia Assemblage B isolate BAH15c1 – Wielinga et al.

RNA Sequence

  • Trophozoite Transcriptome of Giardia assemblage A strain AS175 – Trophozoite transcriptome of G. Assemblage A strain AS175 at passage 4 and 33. These data were aligned to Giardia Assemblage A isolate WB. Franzen et al.

New features in GiardiaDB 44

  • Synteny and the Protein Browser are availabe in our beta implementation of the JBrowse Genome Browser.
  • New EuPathDB RNA Seq Tool. The EuPathDB Galaxy Workspace now offers a tool that converts read counts from HTSeq-count output to FPKM values.
  • New EuPathDB Workflows. The EuPathDB Galaxy Workspace is updated with several new pre-configured workflows that are designed to allow export of strand specific data to EuPathDB for visualization and searching.
Note: Our bimonthly database releases incorporate new data and correct old data when necessary. Changes in annotation and new experimental data may slightly alter your search results by increasing or decreasing the number of hits.
