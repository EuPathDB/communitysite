--- layout: post title: GBrowse added to ToxoDB category: [ToxoDB] --- GBrowse is an interactive generic genome browser developed as part of the GMOD project (www.gmod.org). GBrowse is used in ToxoDB to display gene models, EST alignments, SNPs, Sage tags and BlastZ similarities of GT1 contigs against reference ME49 strain. With GBrowse, we now provide:
  • Custom restriction site identification
  • Open reading frame identification
  • Custom downloads in various formats:
    • FASTA
    • GFF (2)
    • GFF3
    • BSML (XML)
    • EMBL
    • GenBank
    • GAME
    • GCG
